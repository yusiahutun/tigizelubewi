Family Plans, Do They Want Children?

 Posted on February 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  497 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Family plans! While there aren’t any Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift kids in the pipeline just yet, multiple reports seem to suggest that the NFL star and pop sensation are already talking about this major next step. And one member of the family is especially excited about it: Travis Kelce‘s mom, Donna Kelce.

During the December 29, 2023 episode of the “New Heights” podcast, hosted by Travis and his brother Jason Kelce, the brothers invited their mom, Donna Kelce, back to the show. Donna, who previously spoke out about favoring Jason due to his role as a doting father to three adorable daughters with wife Kylie, sparked the age-old sibling rivalry when she admitted that the Eagles player might still be her “favorite.”

“Has that changed at all this season?” Travis asked, to which Donna replied, “Not yet!” with a laugh. Travis couldn’t help but laugh along, quipping, “Ah, the burn!” Mama Kelce quickly smoothed things over, however, telling her sons, “I love you both just the same.”

Despite the good-natured teasing, it seems Travis is taking matters into his own hands to secure that coveted “favorite son” status. Jason and Kylie might be ahead in the grandkid department with three little ones, but Travis, who is currently sans offspring, has been making comments that indicate he’s ready to make the move toward fatherhood.

Back in February 2023, Travis made some controversial comments about needing to “find a breeder” to bring children into the picture. “I’m gonna find a breeder, and I’m gonna get kids so that mom can love me again,” he said on the February 9th episode of his podcast. Little did we know that just a few months later, Travis would start making headlines for his budding romance with none other than Taylor Swift.

The real plot twist, however, came in October 2023 when reports surfaced claiming that Travis and Taylor were not only serious as a couple, but were already making plans for the future. According to a source who spoke to Life & Style, the couple is already contemplating parenthood. “They’re already making plans for the future,” a source revealed to the publication at the time. “Travis wants them in the next year or so, and Taylor, of course, is all in. He’s exactly the guy she’s been waiting for!”

The insider went on to spill the beans about a heartfelt confession Travis made to the “Cruel Summer” singer that supposedly changed the course of their relationship. “He told her that this wasn’t going to be just a fling. He said he can see himself marrying Taylor.”

So, it seems that not only is Travis ready to add some Kelce genes to the family mix, but he might be on the path to becoming Donna’s favorite son after all. Only time will tell if wedding bells and baby rattles will be the next chapters in this unexpected “Love Story,” but we’ll be sure to update you if they do!
