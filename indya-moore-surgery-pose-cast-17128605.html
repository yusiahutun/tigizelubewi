Outfit on point! Indya Moore exposes entire chest in completely see-through dress

 Posted on March 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  339 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The stunning star rose to fame as Angel Evangelista in the hit FX television series Pose.

But Indya Moore looked anything but angelic as they stepped out on the red carpet at the Time 100 Gala in New York on Tuesday.

Ensuring all eyes were on them, the 24-year-old – who identifies as non-binary, meaning they don't define themselves exclusively as masculine or feminine and prefer they/them pronouns – put on a risqué display in a completely sheer gown.

Indya slipped into a shoulderless floor-length number with extravagant ruffle detailing on the sleeves and hemline.

The attention-grabbing frock featured shades of cream and copper throughout and added a sexy touch thanks to its sheer front.

Indya looked confident and relaxed as they exposed their bare assets for awaiting photographers at the event.

They styled their dark tresses into a braided down ‘do, accessorising with a pair of statement diamond earrings.

As for makeup, they opted for a sleek and sophisticated look.

Indya donned a slick of copper eyeshadow with lashings of mascara and a slick of deep red lipstick.

Their latest outing comes shortly after they were branded one of TIME magazine’s 100 most influential LGBT people.

Indya spoke about how grateful they are to be a part of Pose, which has made history as the scripted series with the most transgender actors in regular roles.

They told TIME: “I feel really proud to be a part of such a groundbreaking project and to be able to contribute to it.

“It’s very personal for me. I’m representing an identity that a lot of people have.

“It hits a really personal space because that was me, and in some ways, it still is.”

They added: “I’m really proud of the way that Pose has brought people’s families together and touched people’s hearts and opened people’s minds. It’s really incredible to see. It’s a show about love and family and it highlights what it really means to have a family and to be a family and to love your family.”
