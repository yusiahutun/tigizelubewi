Marital Status

DeVon Franklin is married to Shazam! actress Meagan Good.

Some Other Facts

  • DeVon Franklin is a Southern California University graduate with a bachelor's degree in Business Administration with a major in Entrepreneurship and a minor in Cinema-Television.
