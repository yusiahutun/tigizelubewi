OnlyFans star Astrid Wett nearly gave non-subscribers a glimpse of the good stuff after her top was almost tugged off during a brawl at a recent press conference.

The social media influencer, who became a boxer in 2022, was in attendance to watch fellow influencer-turned-boxer KSI square up to Joe Fournier ahead of their clash at the Wembley Arena on Saturday.

A stir was caused when Wett, who is signed with KSI's Misfits Boxing, got into a scrap with rival Alexia Grace, with the former narrowly avoiding a wardrobe malfunction after punches - and glasses of water - began flying.
