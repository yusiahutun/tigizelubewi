List of the most expensive necklaces in the world in 2021

The most expensive necklaces in the world are considered the most fashionable jewellery items, usually worn by those in the upper class. Their value stems from their good quality, the rarity and weight of their stones, and their precious metals. Here is a list of the costly necklaces in 2021:

1. Heritage in Bloom - $200 million

This lavish necklace holds the record of the most expensive diamond necklace in the world selling for $200 million. It was designed by a Chinese master jeweller by the name Wallace Chan in 2015. What makes this masterpiece even more unique is that it can be disassembled and then put back together again in 27 unique combinations.

This dazzling luxury necklace features 19 more perfectly colourless and internally flawless diamonds, 72 white mutton fat jades, 114 icy green jadeites, 600 pink diamonds. You will be surprised to know that it took a team of 22 jewellers and 11 months of work to complete this masterpiece. The heavy piece of jewellery weighs approximately half a pound.

2. L' incomparable Necklace - $55 million

Second on the list of the most expensive necklaces in the world is L' incomparable. It was considered the most costly necklace until Heritage in Bloom necklace came along. This necklace was designed by Lebanese jeweller Mouawad. It was discovered in a discarded pile of mining rubble by a young girl from Congo in the 1980s

6. Christie's Diamond Necklace - $8.14 million

7. Etcetera's Burmese Ruby necklace - $6.4 million

This necklace is rated as the most expensive ruby-based necklace in history. It was sold for $6.4 million at a Christie's auction in Hong Kong in November 2013. The necklace piece looks like a lace collar and has a total weight of 87.78 carats.

It is enhanced with white pearls, oval diamonds, and rubies. Each ruby used in it comes in diamonds which has created a wave as an added luxury. It is rated among the expensive necklaces because it has many precious diamonds.

8. Mrs. Winston - $5.8 million

This diamond necklace has 7.5 carats of high-quality white diamonds, mostly pearls. In total, the masterpiece necklace has 207 diamonds.

Its precious gems stream down from a diamond band into the middle of the necklace. At the bottom, you will find three large diamonds that add weight to the necklace and complete the jewellery's perfect look.
