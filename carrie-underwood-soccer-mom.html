Pregnant Carrie Underwood Is The Newest Soccer Mom On The Block And She Couldnt Be More Pro

 Posted on June 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  492 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Country music star Carrie Underwood just added a new job to her resume: soccer mom! The singer took to Instagram to announce that she is now a proud soccer mom to her son, Isaiah Fisher, whom she shares with her husband, Mike Fisher.

In the photo — which she looks stunning in, probably thanks to that pregnancy glow — Carrie is seen wearing a warm vest with her blonde locks tied up. The caption read, “Officially a soccer mom this morning! It’s chilly out but I’m staying warm rocking some @caliabycarrie… the kids played a good game! Isaiah scored two goals for his team… and one for the other team. Hey, a goal’s a goal!”

The country music singer, 35, is currently pregnant with baby No. 2, and recently opened up about how she juggles her work life and family life. “Balance is what it’s all about. No matter what your job is,” Carrie told Us Weekly at the 2018 CMT Artists of the Year award show on Oct. 17. “Being a mom, working, and dividing your time — it is what we all have to do as mothers.”

It looks like Carrie may be taking some time off from her busy work schedule to really spend some quality time with her family before they welcome their second child in early 2019, which will then be followed by her Cry Pretty tour beginning in May. Back in April 2017, Carrie admitted to People that her and Mike’s “whole life has changed” since welcoming her three-year-old son, Isaiah. “I remember when we first found out we were gonna have him, it [was] like, ‘How are we gonna do this? Our lives are so crazy as it is’,” Carrie said at the time. “But you just make room and you learn how important that family time is, and to be able to spend time and carve out some of that and maybe get to go on vacation and maybe get to go on a cruise — that stuff is so important to, like I said, make time for family,” she added. “That’s what it’s all about.”

Besides becoming a new soccer player, Isaiah, 3, will be experiencing some more changes within the next few months — like becoming a big brother! “I don’t think for real he understands,” Carrie revealed on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon in September. “He’ll be really sweet and he’ll like, talk to my belly and kiss my belly.”

Carrie dished that Isaiah is not too aware of what is going to happen in a few months when his baby brother or sister is born. “I mean, no kid can really know that their life is going to change,” Carrie told Jimmy. “He’ll just have to share Mommy.” With all these changes happening for Carrie and her family, we can’t wait to see how great of a big brother Isaiah is going to be when his first sibling arrives!
