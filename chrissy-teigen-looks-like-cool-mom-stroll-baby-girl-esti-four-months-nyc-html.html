Chrissy Teigen was the definition of a cool mom while rocking a structured taupe blazer over a white t-shirt on a stroll with her daughter Esti in New York City.

While enjoying her time in the Big Apple on Thursday, the 37-year-old model, who is married to legendary musician John Legend, dutifully pushed her four-month-old baby girl's stroller down the street. 

For the occasion, she wore her freshly blow-dried ginger tresses down and sported a glamorous makeup look, consisting of blush, bronzer and mascara to enhance her natural beauty,

She also revealed she was feeling 'full-body sick' from a week that 'kicked' her 'a**,' but was already on 'the mend.'

The model welcomed Esti with the Grammy-Award winning musician earlier this year via C-section.

Teigen and Legend wed in 2013 after dating on/off for several years. They initially met in 2006 on the set of one of Legend's music videos.

They welcomed their first child, Luna, in April 2016. Son Miles, four, arrived two years later in 2018.

Teigen fell pregnant for a third time in the summer of 2020, although she later had to undergo 'a life-saving abortion.'

She then underwent IVF treatments and revealed in August 2022 that she was pregnant with a baby girl.

Legend recently opened up about his family life and told People that he is proud of his wife for being open about her fertility struggles.
