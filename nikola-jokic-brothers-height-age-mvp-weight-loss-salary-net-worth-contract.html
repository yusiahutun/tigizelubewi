Nikola Jokić MVP

Nikola Jokić became the first Serbian-born player to win MVP and the third player from Europe to win alongside Dirk Nowitzki and Giannis Antetokounmpo. MVP was not the only award Jokic was honored for throughout the season as he was named to his third consecutive all-star team.

Nikola Jokić Weight Loss

While the NBA season was suspended due to the COVID-19 in March 2020, Jokic took the time off to continue working in his fitness in the gym.
