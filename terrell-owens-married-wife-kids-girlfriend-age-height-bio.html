Terrell Owens Bio, Age

The former NFL wide receiver was born Terrell Eldorado Owens on December 7, 1993, in Alabama, USA. Owens was born as the daughter of a 17-year-old woman named Marilyn Heard from Alexander City in Alabama. Marilyn, who had to work double shifts to support her family, moved in with her mother when life became too hard for her because Owens’ father did not support her financially. The young soccer player and his three-infant were eventually trained by her grandmother, a Baptist, who is said to have had a strong connection to the Bible and the bottle.

Terrell Owens did not learn until the age of eleven that the man who lived across the street was his biological father when he fell in love with a girl who lived near them. The father of the little girl, a man in his forties named L.C. Russel made a joke with Owens by telling him that he could not go out with the girl because she was his sister. From his testimony, the boy concluded that he had been living with his father as a neighbor for years without knowing it. Terrell Owens was completely shaken by this information and never ventured near this house again, nor did his father make any conscious attempt to get to know his son.

Owens received his high school education at Benjamin Russel High School, where he was active in basketball, soccer, baseball, and even athletics. He then went to the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, where he recorded excellent college statistics and was honored as a member of the first team of the All-Southern Conference. The 1996 NFL draft favored him when he was selected by the San Francisco 49ers in the third round.

Other Works

Beyond football, Owens has also brought his quota to the entertainment industry, having participated in several business challenges in 2015 along with Donald Trump and other prominent fellow actors in the cast of NBC’s popular television series Celebrity Apprentice. Also in 2015, he swapped lives with famous actor Robert Carradine in another reality television series called “Wife-swapping”.

Terrell Owens Relationships:  Wife, Girlfriends, Kids

At various times in his life, the famous basketball player was romantically involved with several women, including Felisha Terrell, Kari Klinkenborg, Serinda Swan, Kip Force, and Candace Cabrera. His romantic escapades produced two sons, Terique and Atlin, and two daughters, Dasha and Kylee, from four different women, but he was only legally married to Rachel Snider, who worked as a postal worker from Texas. Their marriage, which ended in 2014, ended a few months later due to undisclosed marital problems.

The crux of the matter is that Terrell Owens had to pay up to $45,000 a month in alimony for his children and also spends about $60,000 to support the homes where the various mothers of his children live. Three of his baby mamas attended May 8, 2012, Dr. Phil’s episode to accuse Owens of not meeting his alimony obligations.
