Can Jon Snow outrun the White Walkers in these Adidas Game of Thrones sneakers?

 Posted on April 11, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  191 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Up for grabs are six new patters – each inspired by the Game of Thrones and the colour scheme and moods of the various houses and factions in the series.

There’s enough for everyone in the store, with unique references ranging from the Lannister and Targaryen houses to those of the Night’s Watch. Those seeking allegiance to the White Walker can opt for some chilly blue-and-white sneakers while others can go for the red and black, red and gold or white versions of the luxe sneakers.

The GOT Ultraboost are further prettied up with phrases from the show’s storyline such as “Winter Is Here” and “Hear Me Roar” neatly inscribed on the heel of each shoe. Other features include text on the insoles to branding under the tongue along with the company’s boost midsoles and Primeknit upper- making the sneakers a near perfect deal for GOT aficionados.

The Adidas X Game of Thrones Ultraboost is available on the brand’s website as well as with select retailers for US$180 a pair. Fancy buying yourself a pair?
