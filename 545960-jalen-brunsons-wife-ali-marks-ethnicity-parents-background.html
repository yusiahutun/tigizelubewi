Who is Ali Marks?

She is an American physical therapist born on 10th September 1995 in Lincolnshire, Illinois. Her real name is Alison Pellettieri Marks. Her parents are James and Kathleen Marks. She has two siblings, Danielle and Natalie Marks.

Is Ali Marks Jewish? No. Ali is a Christian. She is of American nationality and has a Caucasian-white ethnic background. Unfortunately, her father passed away in 2010 after succumbing to pancreatic cancer. Ali was only 15 when her father died.

After primary school, Ali attended Stevenson High School, Lincolnshire, Illinois, from 2010 to 2014. She was a member of the National Honors Society and the Student Council. After graduation, she enrolled at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.
