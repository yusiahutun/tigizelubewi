Taylor Swift never fails to keep up sweet tradition after every single album

 Posted on April 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  369 words  |  Martina Birk

Taylor Swift’s sweet tradition with her female celebrity friends are making fans swoon. Kelly Clarkson shares a tradition that Tay Tay has maintained for all these years.

Kelly Clarkson was recently slammed for not supporting Taylor and Travis’ romance but the singer and TV host has clarified that she has been friends with the music legend for a long time. There is no bad blood between them.

Kelly Clarkson reveals sweet tradition that Taylor Swift does every year without fail

Kelly Clarkson has been Taylor Swift‘s friend for a while and Tay Tay never forgets to send her flowers after every album that she releases.

Speaking of the celebrities‘ friendship, Kelly recently claimed: “You know what’s so funny? She just sent me flowers.” 

The 41-year-old singer added: “She’s so nice. She did. She was like, ‘Every time I release something’—’cause she just did 1989. I got that really cute cardigan, too.”

Kelly has also been supportive of Taylor’s business moves over the years. She suggested T. Swizzle to re-record her albums when all of her previous records were sold to Scooter Braun.

Taylor announced her decision to re-record her album later and the Kelly Clarkson Show host praised her smart moves: “She’s a very smart businesswoman.”

Taylor’s female friends over the years

Taylor has gained a star-studded list of friends over the years. Fashion icon Gigi Hadid, TV stars Blake Lively and Sophie Turner, music legends Selena Gomez, and all three Haim sisters have become a part of Tay Tay’s close-knit friends group.

Speaking of Taylor as a friend, Selena shared: “She was frustrated when I was frustrated; she was sad when I was sad.”

Taylor is also the ideal friend who has been there for her friends’ breakups. Recently, she was seen hanging out with Sophie Turner who is going through a divorce from Ex, Joe Jonas.
