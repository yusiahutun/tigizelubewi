Capitol Records Nabs Columbia Exec Andrew Keller For A&amp;R VP Role

 Posted on September 26, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  114 words  |  Martina Birk

NEW YORK (Hypebot) – Andrew Keller has been named VP of A&R for Capitol Records. Keller will sign and develop artists for the company, as well as oversee recording projects for new and established Capitol artists from his base in New York.


Keller joins Capitol after 13 years at Columbia Records, most recently as Director of A&R. He was responsible for signing Krewella, Dillon Francis, ZHU, MS MR. St. Lucia and metal band Bring Me the Horizon while at Columbia.


He began his career as an intern in Columbia's A&R Department in 2003. He graduated from New York University's Gallatin School of Individualized Study in 2007, with a concentration in Art & Commerce.
