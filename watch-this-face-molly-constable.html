Watch This Face: Molly Constable

Alexander Meininger

Every week, Bazaar's booking team highlights one name to note, from the industry's best-known faces to girls who are just starting out. This week, we turn our attention to Molly Constable.

Every season has a breakout model who makes the industry sit up and take note; this time it is Molly Constable. With her piercing gaze, full pout and curvaceous appeal, this American model has captured the attention of fashion's leading players.

Posting unretouched (and often witty) photos, and sharing her personal style on Instagram has made Constable a rising star, but she's more than just another social-media girl. Shooting with the likes of Tom Ford and Tim Walker, and booking the kind of coveted, high-end work that has previously eluded those who are not sample-size, Constable is one of only a handful of models whose chameleon looks can be transformed from luxury fashion, where her delicacy and poise shines, to the higher octane and sexier world of lingerie modelling, giving her a unique position in the industry.

"Molly is truly unique as she has such an editorial look and has such iconic curves," says Anna Shillinglaw at Milk Management. "Molly is getting the whole industry to wake up and see that there is a place for curvier models in high-fashion magazines and campaigns, so she is most definitely one to watch."

We talk to the newcomer about being discovered on Instagram, skateboarding and snowboarding, and getting on the Stair Master.

Alexander Meininger

How were you discovered?

"I was discovered in 2012 through Instagram. I liked a photo posted by my now mother agents, Mother Model, and they ended up reaching out to me on my selfie on my profile asking if I wanted to be a model. Later that week, they sent me to meet with Ford Models in New York and I signed with them the following week. You could say we started the whole 'Instagram model' movement!"

If you weren't modelling, which career would you pursue and why?

"I would love to be a stylist. I love experimenting with crazy pieces and seeing people's reactions."

What would you say has been the highlight of your career so far?

"I think that would have to be shooting with Tim Walker for Love magazine. He is one of the most passionate and kind photographers I have ever worked with. It was also just inspiring being surrounded by some of the most influential models and creative people in the industry right now."

How do you spend your free time?

"I am really into painting. I skateboard around the city in the summer and I love to snowboard in the winter. I also enjoy spending a lot of time searching for crazy vintage pieces."

Alexander Meininger

Do you have any travel tips for those visiting your hometown?

"I'm from a very small town in upstate New York. Walking isn't really an option since everything is very spaced out, you will definitely need a car. There is Hunter mountain, which I grew up snowboarding on, and in the summer they host a lot of fun events. There're also amazing little restaurants and a lot of antique and vintage shops around."

What are the three products you can't live without?

"Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray, Nuxe 24hr Hydrating and Smoothing Cream and my Anastasia eyebrow pencil."

What is your favourite form of exercise?

"My friend recently got me addicted to the Stair Master; granted, I can't go long without begging for mercy, but that's always great for thy booty. I usually focus on my butt and abs, but if I'm really in the training mood I will use the Nike training app. It is a really amazing app that helps you focus on exactly which area you want to hit. There's also step-by-step demonstrations that are extremely helpful."

What is your dream shoot?

"My dream shoot would be a cover for a magazine in some crazy location like the middle of a glacier in Iceland; to work with Mert & Marcus or Terry Richardson would be cool."
