You can create a fan page for various reasons; for an online store, your favourite celebrity, a favourite show or certain individual personalities. The page name should be catchy since you're hoping to attract more social media users by the username that you choose. So, do you have fan page username ideas?

A fan page username idea for Instagram should not be too long. Strive to be unique in the name that you choose. The page should have a few characters and no numbers, if possible. Adding numbers to a fan page username makes it less appealing.

If you have to add extra characters to a username, settle with an underscore, as underscores do not take away the aesthetics of a username. Also, consider the shortened version of the name, as short names are easy to look up. A name like 'Rebecca the Artist' will look better if shortened to 'Becca Arts'
