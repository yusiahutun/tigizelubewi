Katherine Izzo Job

Katherine Izzo is Clinic Registered Nurse. In 2023, she was working at Moffitt Cancer Center for almost 2 years.

Prior to that, Katherine worked as a travel nurse at St. Joseph’s Hospital for 5 months, Brandon Regional Hospital for 4 months, and Massachusetts General Hospital for 4 months. Also, she was a staff registered nurse at Tampa General Hospital from Jun 2020 to Nov 2020, a Surgical Transplant Progressive Care nurse at Advent Health from Oct 2018 to  May 2020, and a certified nursing assistant at Orlando Health from Dec 2017 to Jun 2018.

As for her education, she got her BSN from the University of Central Florida in 2019. Later, she then studied MSN and FNP at Southern University from 2020 to 2022.

Also, Katherine has certifications in Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) and Basic Life Support for Healthcare Providers (BLS) from the American Heart Association.

By 2023, she garnered a net worth of under $300 thousand.

For those of you wondering, a Clinic Registered Nurse (Katherine’s job) in the US made around $70 thousand per annum.
