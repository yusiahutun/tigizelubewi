Kylie Jenner Wore a Blue PVC Trench Coat by Saks Potts on Insta

 Posted on September 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  312 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Kylie Jenner announced on Instagram that she's already feeling that "2020 energy."
  • Posing with Stassie Karanikolaou, Kylie looked stunning in a light blue trench, featuring a fur collar.
  • The friends matched in the Saks Potts trench coats.

Kylie Jenner has worn an impressive array of outfits over the festive season, including her custom Ralph & Russo leg slit gown. And just like the rest of us, the makeup mogul is getting ready to see in 2020.

In a new Instagram post, the Kylie Cosmetics founder posed with her close friend, Stassie Karanikolaou. Both women wore striking Saks Potts PVC trench coats with fur collars and cuffs. While Kylie opted for a light and bright blue shade, Karanikolaou rocked neon orange.

Kylie didn't appear to be wearing any clothing underneath her trench coat, either.

This is far from the only revealing look that Kylie has rocked this holiday season. She adorably matched with daughter Stormi at the Kardashian-Jenner Christmas Eve bash. Both mother and daughter wore emerald green gowns, and looked completely regal at the shindig. The billionaire's gown featured a thigh-high leg slit, and she accessorized the dress with a plethora of jewels.

Kylie's ex, Travis Scott, just released "Gatti" with rap collective JACKBOYS. Rapping on the track, Scott appears to reference his split from Kylie Jenner, and suggests that the former couple has very different lifestyles. He revealed, "Duck away, she wanna lay up and hibernate (Yeah) / I took a chance, it’s a lot to take."
