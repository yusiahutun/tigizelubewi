Diamond Platnumz has attracted criticism online after arriving at his recent show in a coffin.

Creativity or clout? Diamond criticised over coffin show

The Tanzanian crooner tried to spice up his entrance for the biggest show he hosted in Ruangwa, Lindi district with a horror theme, but fans did not like his creativity. Many termed his creativity as satanic.
