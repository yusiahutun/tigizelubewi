Christian has dramatically changed since he began his career as a musician in 2016. Not only has he changed as an artist but also physically. Christian has increased the number of tattoos on his body since his breakthrough. But do these body patterns have any significance? Yes, they do. Below is a breakdown of the tattoos on Christian Nodal's face and body.

Face tattoos

How many tattoos does Christian Nodal have on his face? Christian Nodal has over fifteen ink designs on his face. His love led him to ink his face with art, capturing various periods in his career and personal life. For example, the left side of his face comprises ink that expresses certain aspects of his spiritual connections.

The inking on the left side of the face consists of a lilac-coloured flower and the words utopia. The utopia inscription represents paradise and good times, while the purple flower signifies modesty, innocence, virtue, affection, care, and love.

Neck tattoos

Hand tattoos

Christian's love for body art has seen him ink his hands. The star has a tattoo on his knuckles, each significant. The right-hand inking includes an eagle, a green heart, a moon, an eye, a rose and a cross tattoo. According to the artist, these inscriptions deter any ill will that might come his way. The eagle represents comfort and the need to be safe.

The left hand has inscriptions that signify positivity and his roots. The designs include the horseshoe, a sun for good luck, and a cactus plant representing his roots as a Mexican. The musician also has a skull imprint on his hand, believed to be for aesthetic reasons.

The star also spots wrist ink designs with barbed wires and a scarf. These wires signify protection and guidance, providing safety when needed.

Leg tattoos

Compared to the amount of body art on Christian's upper body, the inscriptions on his lower half are low. The musician has two design ink patterns on his right leg and none on his left. The tattoos, including a flower and a cartoon character, represent strength and memories, respectively.
