This could be the creepiest (and most bizarre) horror movie you see in 2017

 Posted on June 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  258 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

But we think this UK comedy horror film has leapfrogged Oscar baiting movies in our must-watch list of 2017.

The trailer for Alice Lowe's Prevenge has dropped, and it could be the most bizarre horror movie of the year.

Alice plays Ruth, a heavily pregnant woman who has lost her boyfriend in apparently tragic circumstances.

However, while grief can take many different forms, we've never seen it done quite like this.

Ruth's baby appears to be telling her to murder people, including DJs in bars and women in offices.

The evil foetus says: "You're getting better at this", as Ruth admits to her nurse: "I'm scared of her. I would swap her to have him back."

And later in the trailer, we hear the baby tell her mum: "Kill him or I kill you."

It is unclear whether Ruth is delusional or her foetus is actually pure evil, but there's a whole lot of gore promised in Prevenge.

Ruth is seeing slitting a woman's throat in a boardroom, slashing a man's leg and attacking a woman in her home with a baseball bat before brandishing a knife.

Alice Lowe, 39, not only plays Ruth, but wrote and directed Prevenge as well.

However, the best part is that Alice – who has appeared in Garth Marenghi's Darkplace and Sightseers – was actually seven months pregnant when she played the mum-to-be/murderer in the film.

The film premiered at Cannes earlier this year to rave reviews.

Prevenge is set for release in February 2017.
