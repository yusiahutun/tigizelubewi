7 celebrity brides and their Kate Middleton inspired wedding dresses

 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  503 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The Duchess of Cambridge's dress has made history

    It has been 11 years since Kate Middleton walked down the aisle at Westminster Abbey to marry the Queen's grandson Prince William. Her iconic wedding dress has gone down in history and it continues to be a huge source of inspiration for brides.

    MORE: Kate Middleton's secret change to Princess Diana's £123k engagement ring

    The Duchess of Cambridge opted for a timeless gown from Sarah Burton of Alexander McQueen with long lace sleeves and a full skirt – much like Grace Kelly.

    Have you noticed that the likes of Kim Kardashian and Kelly Clarkson have donned stunning bridal outfits that have all the same vibes as Kate's? Take a look at beautiful royal-inspired looks over the past 11 years…

    WATCH: Look Back At Prince William And Kate Middleton's Royal Wedding

    Kim Kardashian

    star Kim Kardashian tied the knot with Kanye West in 2014 in a custom mermaid gown by Givenchy. Although the sheer lace back and waist are different to Kate's, there's no mistaking the similarities of the long lace sleeves and impressive veil.

    Rose Leslie

    star Rose Leslie certainly looked like royalty when she married Kit Harrington. The actress opted for a long-sleeved Elie Saab gown which boasted a boat neckline and intricate floral embroidery, but her open back was a slightly more daring take on Kate's dress.

    Jacqueline Jossa

    View post on Twitter

    Jacqueline Jossa's bespoke Suzanne Neville gown was based on the 'Matisse' style from the Suzanne Neville Portrait Collection. The fitted corset and long-sleeve beaded lace jacket were reminiscent of Kate's silhouette. For the evening celebrations, the actress removed the jacket to reveal a strapless dress underneath.

    Nicky Hilton

    Nicky Hilton looked stunning in her Valentino dress on her wedding day to James Rothschild in 2015. Her high-neck lace gown was embellished with crystals and finished with a statement train – three metres long, compared to Kate's 2.7 metres.

    Kelly Clarkson

    View post on Twitter

    When Kelly Clarkson and Brandon Blackstock eloped in 2013, the singer wore a long-sleeve lace wedding dress by Temperley. The pretty gown features a V-neckline, much like Kate's, a low back and embellishments.

    Karlie Kloss

    How beautiful was Karlie Kloss' Dior wedding dress? As she said 'I do' to Jared Kushner, the model rocked a custom dress with a V-neckline, fitted waist and lace sleeves.

    Nikki Reed

    View post on Instagram 

    's Nikki Reed looked stunning on her wedding day tostar Ian Somerhalder in 2015. A modern twist on the Duchess' dress, her lace gown by Claire Pettibone was backless with three-quarter length sleeves.
