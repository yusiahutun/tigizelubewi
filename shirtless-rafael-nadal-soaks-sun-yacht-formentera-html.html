He dramatically pulled out of Wimbledon last week on the eve of his semi-final against Nick Kyrgios with an abdominal tear.

And Rafael Nadal was taking some time out to relax after the disappointing tournament as he enjoyed a holiday in Formentera on Monday.

The 36-year-old Spaniard showed off his sporty physique as he soaked up the sun on board a yacht and enjoyed a jet ski ride.
