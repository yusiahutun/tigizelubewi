Inside JLo's birthday at $60 million mansion - wait until you see her $6k Kaftan

 Posted on June 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  470 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Although our invite to JLo’s party may have gotten lost in the post, she’s now shared a sneak peek into her birthday party hosted by her husband Ben Affleck, at their new Beverley Hills mansion. However, it may have been her designer bikini, and kaftan that stole the spotlight.

The music star has been on the scene since the 90s, and with almost three decades of birthdays under the spotlight, this one may be a contender for one of the best yet. We take a look inside.

JLo shares ‘peek’ into her birthday party

Jennifer looked to be having the time of her life as she danced the night away on the table surrounded by her loved ones to Lizzo’s About Damn Time.

She stunned in a  backless, silver dress and although her husband Ben couldn’t be seen clearly in the reel, the hit singer also attached some photos for fans, with her arm around him. The pair also recently celebrated their 1st wedding anniversary.

“Birthday mood… all month!! It’s always a good day when Lola comes to play…” Lopez captioned the letter to fans.

The newly turned 54-year-old thanked her family and friends in the letter as she wrote:  

“I felt so appreciated and acknowledged by my loved ones, and I’m extremely grateful to be where I am today at this point in my life’s journey with so many beautiful, caring people to share it all with.

Wishing you all the same love and happiness on your birthday and always!!!”

The star previously showed off her birthday looks via Instagram, as fans wished her many happy returns.

JLo shows off her toned body in designer bikini

In one of the exclusive videos shared to her mailing list, JLo showed off her toned bikini body as she posed at her birthday party in a black Panther Valentino bikini and matching kaftan, costing $5,900.00. (Swipe the above pic to see the $6k in all its glory!)

The star was standing in front of a gorgeous view and infinity pool as she strutted towards the camera showing off her abs.

In the newsletter, the Jenny From The Block singer wrote: “All the kids were there, it was a gorgeous day full of sunshine and perfect for a pool party!!”

Inside Ben and JLo’s new $60 million home

Ben and JLo’s home was reportedly bought by the couple for $60 million at the start of June and is located in Beverley Hills, California.

The celebrity couple’s 38,000-square-foot new home includes a whopping 12 bedrooms, 24 bathrooms, and a full basketball court, as per Page Six.

“The home also has a zero-edge pool, a guest penthouse, a caretaker’s house, a 12-car garage, and enough room to park a staggering 80 cars on the lot,” the publication also states.
