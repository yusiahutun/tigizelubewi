Kevin Hart is a well-known comedian and actor. His stand-up performances are frequently breathtaking and awe-inspiring.

14. Charlie Sheen - Patek Philippe Reference 2438/1 with Moonphase ($700,000)

Charlie Sheen is an American actor and producer who rose to fame in the 1980s. He became well-known after appearing in Two and a Half Men. Charlie has always been an avid watch collector, particularly of Patek Philippe watches.

He is the proud owner of a Patek Philippe Perpetual Calendar Ref. 2438/1. This rare timepiece features a day/date complication, a moon phase dial, and outer Arabic five-minute divisions. The watch is valued at approximately $700,000.

13. Ellen Degeneres - Rolex Daytona "John Player" Special ($728,000)

Ellen Lee DeGeneres is an American comedian, television host, actress, writer, and producer. She has been active in her profession since 1978. The television host owns the Rolex Daytona "John Player" Special worth $728,000.

It is one of the most expensive watches made worldwide. It was nicknamed after the livery of John Player's '70s Lotus in Formula 1. This Rolex is the watch collector catnip. Therefore, it fits that Degeneres, a known watch lover, would have one in her collection.

What is the most expensive watch in the world?

The Breguet Grande Complication Marie-Antoinette is one of the most expensive watches in the world, worth $30 million. It is truly a stunning timepiece, and its intricate craftsmanship is remarkable. It features a range of complications such as a tourbillon, perpetual calendar, minute repeater, and split-seconds chronograph.

Which brand is the most expensive watch?

The most expensive watch brand is Patek Philippe. It is known for crafting some of the world's most luxurious and valuable watches. Their watches are made with the finest materials and are known for their intricate designs, making them highly sought after. They are often seen as a status symbols and can easily cost thousands of dollars.
