It's a four-leaved clover for luck - Camilla, Kate and even Molly-Mae are wearing Van Cleef &amp; Arpels

 Posted on January 28, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  651 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Queen Camilla already had an impressive collection of jewels at her disposal before her husband became King. And that collection will surely grow in the coming years and months.

Yet we can expect to see her to continue to use a favourite set of jewels: her numerous pieces from Van Cleef & Arpels. 

Camilla has been wearing them for many years. She has numerous pieces from the brand, including earrings, necklaces as well as bracelets. But she clearly prefers the Alhambra range that encompasses lucky-clover style motifs. 

The Queen Consort is rarely without her bracelets or necklaces, which manage to be light yet bold. On special occasions she turns to her beautiful Magic Alhambra diamond earrings.

Camilla attended a charity launch at the V&A wearing her Van Cleef and Arpels matching earrings and necklace in 2017

Camilla wore her Magic Alhambra Diamond earrings from Van Cleef & Arpels, at the Man Booker Prize award ceremony in October 2015

Camilla greeting Geri Horner at a 2022 Buckingham Palace reception wearing the blue and gold Magic Alhambra bracelet

Kate Middleton has worn her Magic Alhambra set set twice. Kate wore the Magic Alhambra Earrings with two motifs which were created in 2006 together with the matching Magic Alhambra necklace with different sized Alhambra motifs all in a different size. 

Kate first wore her Van Cleef & Arpels 'Alhambra' set at the Baftas in 2020 and more recently when she stunned the world in her ice blue Phillipa Lepley gown in the Bahamas in 2022 when she was touring the Caribbean with Prince William.

The Love Island star, Molly-Mae Hague revealed that she had been gifted a £3,850 Van Cleef & Arpels bracelet from her boyfriend Tommy Fury on her 22nd birthday in 2021. 

She went on to wear a blue Van Cleef and Arpels Alhambra necklace which is worth around £15,000 round her neck and then as a belly chain in Mexico.

On holiday last year in with her 'fiancée' Rich Paul, Adele spent time on a yacht in Sardinia and was clad in casual, carefree fashion and accessories, accented by an armful of Van Cleef & Arpels colorful Alhambra bracelets mixed with yellow gold thin cuffs and a special charm on her other wrist. 

Jack Grealish's partner, Sasha Attwood 27, who is a successful model and influencer  wears not one, but two Van Cleef & Arpels bracelets. She appears to be wearing the 18k yellow gold bracelet which costs £3,750, and the blue version, which is £4,350.
