Laurel Lord will bless Israel | Letters to the Editor

 Posted on June 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  58 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Lord said this about Israel-I will make you into a great nation and I will bless you: “I will make your name great, and you will be a blessing. I will bless those who bless you, and whoever curses you I will curse; and all peoples on earth will be blessed through you.”

F. Handel Craft
