Adam Gawlas tips entire bottle of water over head to celebrate making UK Open semi-final

 Posted on February 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  300 words  |  Martina Birk

Adam Gawlas had an interesting celebration after he made the semi-finals of the UK Open on Sunday.

Gawlas shocked the world with the biggest win of his professional career as he knocked off Rob Cross in the quarter-finals. The Czech was 9-8 up when he needed 83 to check out and take the game, booking a spot in the final four.

He missed his first chance to check out, but Cross handed him a golden opportunity when he fluffed his lines. And Gawlas, after nailing the double 16, celebrated by pouring some water all over his face - in scenes the fans loved.

One fan joked: "How p***** is Adam Gawlas? 10 pints of Staropramen before that match!"

"What a win for Adam Gawlas and what a celebration," another added with a number of laughing emojis. While a third tweeted: "Well played Gawlas, fantastic win for him, the celebration afterwards was almost better than his darts lol!"

And another joked, after seeing a video of his celebration: "Ok I’m now an Adam Gawlas fan!"

It's not the first time Gawlas has rolled out this celebration - and he performed the same stunt at the PDC World Championships in December.

Clearly overcome with emotion, Gawlas grabbed the first thing he could see - a pint of water - and poured it all over his head.
