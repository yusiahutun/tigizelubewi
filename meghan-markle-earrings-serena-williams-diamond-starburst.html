Meghan Markle wore Serena Williams' $179 diamond earrings - and now they're back in stock

 Posted on June 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  375 words  |  Martina Birk

We love the Duchess of Sussex’s starburst earrings from BFF Serena’s jewelry line

    How chic did Meghan Markle look when she stepped out for the Invictus Games in the Netherlands in 2022, rocking a classic black tweed jacket, jeans, and diamond earrings from BFF Serena Williams’ jewelry line? 

    The Serena Williams Jewelry $179 (£146) diamond starburst stud earrings were the perfect accessory for the outfit - and now the sold out look is back in stock! 

    The star-shaped earrings from Serena Williams’ jewelry line (which also created the tennis champion’s G.O.A.T. necklace) are made from 10k white gold and encrusted with 1/10 carats of pavé diamonds. 

    “These bright stars will sparkle up your #wfh sweats as well as your night-out LBD,” reads the brand’s website.

    Starburst Stud Earrings, $179 / £146 at Serena Williams Jewelry

    Shoppers have given the earrings a 4.75-star of 5 overall rating. “LOVE these beauties. So very comfortable and pretty. And affordable!” said one. Another raved: “I love the earrings. They go with everything that I wear and the diamonds sparkle!”

    When Duchess Meghan first rocked the earrings in 2022, the jewelry brand’s Instagram account shared the news with a snap of the royal mom-of-two captioned: “We spot the Duchess of Sussex rocking our Diamond Starburst earrings,” followed by a magnifying glass and eyeball emojis.

    Fans who wanted the earrings were soon out of luck though, after the pretty gems sold out. “Please consider bringing these earrings back in stock. They would probably sell out very quickly,” said one commenter.

    Well, as luck would have it, Serena has brought the earrings back! And we predict that once word gets out they’ll be flying off the virtual shelves.

    So if you want the Meghan and Serena-approved diamond earrings, we recommend grabbing a pair ASAP…
