Beauty entrepreneur Anastasia Soare is the founder and CEO of the cosmetic brand, Anastasia Beverly Hills; she launched her namesake brand in 1997 and introduced her first product line in 2000. She was the first to introduce the brow shaping to American salons.

In 2019, Anastasia was ranked as 21st on Forbes lists of America's Self-Made Women. Today, Anastasia's company sells more than 480 products in over 3,000 stores.
