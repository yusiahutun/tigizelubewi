Taylor Swift fans tingle in excitement for new music as singer spotted in recording studio

 Posted on June 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  425 words  |  Martina Birk

Taylor Swift fans have a lot to look forward to in the new year, including the possibility of new music from the queen of pop.

The Miss Americana and Heartbreak Princess has previously teased her upcoming album, Reputation Taylor’s Version last year. She revealed the vault tracks would embody female rage in a major hint. While no release date for Reputation TV has been hinted at yet, Taylor Swift‘s recent arrival at a recording studio at the beginning of the year only teases even more, big things are coming.

Taylor Swift fans speculate ‘new music’ is coming

Whether a new album or Reputation’s Taylor Version, Swift heading into the recording studio is a big teaser she could be back with new music to the table. The 34-year-old celebrity was spotted at the Electric Lady recording studio in Greenwich Village this week.

She was seen wearing knitted emerald green jumper dress, long brown coat and black boots. She paired the ensemble with a Yves Saint Laurent handbag. Her hair cascaded naturally down her shoulders and Taylor, of course, had her signature red lip.

The singer’s latest few looks have one thing in common – the color green. For Swifties, the color green is a nod to the snake era aka Reputation. They’ve decoded this could mean she’s re-recording the album now.

As Taylor Swift is gearing up to return to her record-breaking Eras tour in February, fans are eager to know if they will get the most-awaited news of Reputation Taylor’s Version dropping before that.

Speaking of the exciting time ahead, a fan commented on X (formerly Twitter): “I cannot wait to listen to what she has been working on, it’s going to be massive.”

All about Reputation TV

Before the New Year, Taylor Swift sat down with Time for a profile piece after being named their Person Of The Year. She talked about her upcoming Reputation, Taylor’s Version.

The Bad Blood crooner loves to build momentum before a big drop, and this was no difference. She teased the vault tracks for Reputation will be “fire.”

“It’s a goth-punk moment of female rage at being gaslit by an entire social structure,” she revealed. “I think a lot of people see it, and they’re just like, sick snakes and strobe lights.”

Sticking to her Reputation theme, the Ready For It crooner also shared she is collecting ‘horcruxes.’

Taylor added: “I’m collecting infinity stones. Gandalf’s voice is in my head every time I put out a new one. For me, it is a movie now.”
