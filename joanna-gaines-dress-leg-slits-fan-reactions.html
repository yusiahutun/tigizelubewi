'Fixer Upper' Star Joanna Gaines Shuts Down the 'Tonight Show' in Lace Dress

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  297 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

For Fixer Upper stars Chip and Joanna Gaines, this Valentine's Day was a special one! The parents of five children spent February 14 in New York City and made a rare appearance on a nighttime talk show.

Chip and Jo were guests on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon, and they talked about Joanna's new memoir, The Stories We Tell. The couple, who celebrates their 20th wedding anniversary this year, explained that they typically spend the holiday at home with their kids, getting dressed up and eating a fancy dinner. But this year, they spent the evening telling charming stories about their lives, including the story of how they met and the time they met Taylor Swift and acted "like idiots," as Chip explained.

Joanna shared several playful photos from the event on Instagram. She wrote, "Had so much fun celebrating Valentine's Day here in NYC and on @fallontonight! Thanks for having us @jimmyfallon we had the best time! ❤️"

NBC

For the appearance, a clean-shaven Chip wore a stylish blazer and white button-down shirt, while Joanna wore a black lace halter dress with daring leg slits. Fans left comments showing the love for the couple and their looks.
