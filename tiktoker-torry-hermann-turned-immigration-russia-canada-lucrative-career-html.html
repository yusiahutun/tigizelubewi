TikToker Torry Hermann turned her immigration from Russia to Canada into a lucrative career

 Posted on May 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  493 words  |  Martina Birk

When she was fired from her job at a car dealership in Canada, Russian immigrant Torry Hermann was left scratching her head over what to do next.

Little did the hilarious influencer know, getting axed from the role (on her birthday!) would lead to her dream career: a TikToker whose videos have become legendary to both women and the Russian immigrant community.

Torry has 3million followers and 4billion social media views, thanks to her comical videos in which she shares her experiences since she bravely left her native Russia aged 15 to move to Canada.

Torry Hermann turned her bad luck into a lucrative career after getting fired from her job at a car dealership - she turned her Russian vs Canadian immigration story into a TikTok viral hit

Upon her arrival in Canada, she got a job at a car dealership where her confidence, enthusiasm and creativity helped her become a top sales reps in Vancouver, leading to her using social media to make wildly-popular car videos.

Despite the videos gaining traction, she was shocked to discover she had been fired and after starting a fruitless job hunt, she decided to go full time with social media, comedy videos and content creation.

She soon went viral with her Russian vs. American skits, in which she shared her experiences with living abroad and also reached out to women.

Torry recently married Hollywood media producer Vito Glazers, and they have since created a series of videos around immigrant and couple comedy which have garnered over 100million views in the past year.

Her content exploded particularly with audiences of immigrant and European women from around the world and she has now become an extremely desirable asset for advertisers thanks to her vast audience.

Torry, who recently launched her Sexy Puma brand, boasts 90% women between the ages of 25-45 as her audience – a rare feat.

Sexy Puma aims to empower women globally and includes everything from jewellery to clothing, female empowerment coaching, perfume, live events, and more.

According to Torry, who has authored several books to help women overcome fears and challenges, her goal is to help women unlock their full potential.

Following her recent move from Canada to LA, Torry has a host of projects in the pipeline including a movie being made from her first screenplay and a continuation of her work in comedy

Despite constant calls from promoters, she admits she is not ready to hit the stage for stand-up yet: ‘I don’t want to get on stage just yet, I’m very happy just making my TikToks’.
