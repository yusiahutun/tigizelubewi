Parris Renee Goebel MNZM (; born 29 October 1991), also known mononymously as Parris, is a New Zealand dancer and choreographer. She is the founder and main choreographer of the Palace Dance Studio, which has produced dance crews such as ReQuest, Sorority, Bubblegum, and the Royal Family. The latter has won the World Hip Hop Dance Championship three times in a row, becoming the first dance crew in history to achieve it.
