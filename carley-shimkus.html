Shimkus keeps her personal and work life separate. She has managed to keep a balance between the two from the very beginning. This B.A. graduate knows how to handle many responsibilities side by side.

 

Career 

 

2009 Shimkus graduated with a B.A. in broadcast journalism from Quinnipiac University. During her senior year, she managed to get an internship at FOX Business Network. After school, she worked as a news producer at Fox News, Which she made a good decision about because earlier she wanted to be a model.


In 2012, she was promoted to associate producer with the FOX Business Network, working for Imus in the Morning. She then moved to the on-air side of the business, where she serves as a headlines reporter for Fox & Friends and Fox & Friends First and appears on Fox Nation.


With elegance and grace, she has managed to deliver daily news impressively. 

Not only in the news, but Carley has also appeared in many online videos for Fox News Magazine. In 2016, she did several videos called My Big Fat Digital Wedding, where she advises brides.
