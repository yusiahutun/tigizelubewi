Who is Mark Putnam?

Mark Steven Putnam was born on 4 July 1959, in Coventry, Connecticut, United States, to Barbara (mother) and Walter (father). He was raised alongside his two younger siblings.

His dad worked as a truck driver for Sears, while his mother was a full-time stay-at-home mother. The former FBI special agent attended the University of Tampa as a B-average student majoring in criminology.

In August 1982, he became a clerk at the FBI's Connecticut headquarters office, working as a telephone switchboard operator during night shifts and weekends.

He held his clerical position at the FBI until 1986, when he took the entrance exam for the FBI Academy. In October 1986, he graduated and was eventually rewarded with the gold FBI badge.
