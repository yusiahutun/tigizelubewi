The hunk had women head-over-heels as he stormed in with his abs and light-hearted banter.

One user shared a shot of him and wrote: "I mean the boys aren’t even bad looking on Love Island, but they’re just not Jack Fowler."

While another added: "I don’t think there will ever be a more attractive Islander than Jack Fowler to me, that man is devine."

A third fan chirped: "Waiting for someone who is as fire as Jack Fowler, Alex Bowen and Wes Nelson to walk in and steal my heart."
