How to Decorate with Clear Acrylic Side Tables

Due to its transparent design, a modern acrylic side table can be used just about anywhere in your home. And, this versatility is one of the main reasons why clear design furniture has become so popular in recent years.

Here are some ideas on how to decorate your spaces with this piece of furniture.

1. Living Room

Acrylic furniture can make your living room look larger and more spacious. This is because it doesn’t visually clutter the room as heavier pieces do. So, if you want to open up your living room and make it appear bigger, a side table is a great option.

One good way to decorate your living room with this piece of furniture is by creating a unique focal point. For example, you can use acrylic end tables to showcase your favorite art pieces or family photos. Or, you can place one near your door to create a more welcoming entryway.

If you have extra floor space, then you can also create a coffee nook. All you need is a clear acrylic coffee table and some comfortable chairs. This is a great way to create an intimate seating area where you can relax and enjoy your cup of coffee.

You can then add some accent pieces to make the area more inviting. For example, you can place a rug under the coffee table and add some throw pillows on the chairs. You can also add a few decorative items around, such as candles or vases.

2. Bedroom

A clear acrylic side table is also great for bedrooms. You can use it as a bedside table or a nightstand.

If you want to save space, then you can opt for clear acrylic nesting tables. These are perfect for small bedrooms as they can be stored away easily when not in use.

Another good way to decorate your bedroom with clear acrylic side tables is by using them as a dressing table. This will give you a clear surface where you can do your makeup or get ready for the day. You can then add a stool or chair so you can sit comfortably while you get ready.

To complete the look, you can add some decorative items, such as a vase of fresh flowers or some scented candles.

3. Dining Room

There are many ways to use a clear design side table in your dining room and add interest to the space. For starters, you can use it as a buffet table. This will give you a clear surface to place food and drinks when entertaining guests.

Another good way to decorate your dining room with this type of furniture is by using it as a bar cart. This can be pretty useful when you host parties and other special occasions in your home. You can stock the cart with all the necessary items, such as glasses, bottles, and mixers, and when it’s time to entertain, simply roll the cart out and let your guests help themselves!

Apart from a side table, you can also add in some clear acrylic shelves to display your best dinnerware or glassware. This is a good way to maximize storage and keep your dining room looking neat and organized.

4. Hallway

If you have a dark hallway, then you can use an acrylic accent table to make it more stylish and inviting. In a way, it helps make the space feel brighter and more open.

One good idea is to use it as a console table. This will give you a clear surface where you can place a few decorative items, such as vases, photo frames, or sculptures. You can also add a lamp to create a warm and inviting ambiance.

This type of table is also the perfect spot to place things like keys, mail, or sunglasses when you come home.

5. Bathroom

A fascinating way to decorate your bathroom with this piece of furniture is by using it as a makeshift vanity. Simply place a mirror on top of the table and add some lighting. Then, you can use it just like you would a regular vanity!

Another idea is to use such a table as a storage unit. Just pick one with shelves or drawers and use it to store all your bathroom essentials. This way, you can keep the countertop clear and clutter-free.

You can also use it as a towel rack. You can roll up some towels and place them on the table.

As you can see, there are so many ways to decorate your home with acrylic side tables! So, don’t be afraid to get creative and experiment until you find the perfect look for your space. Now, all you need to do is find the perfect piece for your needs.

How to Pick the Perfect Modern Acrylic Side Table for Your Needs

 As previously implied, acrylic side tables come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors. So, how do you know which one is the best fit for your needs? Here are some factors to keep in mind.

Size

One of the first things you need to consider is the size of the table. You need to make sure that it’s not too big or too small for the space you have in mind. It also needs to be proportional to the other pieces of furniture in the room.

So, before you start shopping, take some measurements and make a note of the dimensions you need. This way, you can narrow down your options and save yourself some time.

Shape

Acrylic side tables come in a variety of shapes, such as round, square, or rectangular. They can even come in irregular shapes! So, think about which shape would work best in the space you have.

For example, a round acrylic side table might be perfect for a small nook and goes well with a curved sofa. If you have a large open space, then a rectangular table would be a good choice.

If you want to make a statement, then you can go for those with irregular shapes, such as polygons, urn shapes, and those which look like letters!

Style

Acrylic tables come in different styles such as modern, vintage, or minimalist. So, think about the overall aesthetic of the room and choose a table that fits in with the rest of the decor.

If you want something that can create a cohesive look, then go for an acrylic side table with chrome legs. For a more understated look, on the other hand, you can pick one with wood or glass shelves.

Hue

You should also consider the color of the table. If you want something that stands out, then go for a brightly colored table. But if you prefer a more subdued look, then choose a clear or white acrylic side table, instead.

You can also find tables in different shades of black, grey, or brown. So, take your time and browse through all the different options until you find the perfect one for your home.

Material

Generally speaking, modern acrylic furniture pieces are made ofLucite orplexiglass.

Lucite is a clear, colorless plastic that’s used in high-end furniture. It’s shatter-resistant and has a high level of clarity.

Plexiglass, on the other hand, is less expensive than Lucite, but it still has a high level of clarity. It also doesn’t yellow over time like some other plastics.

Your choice would boil down to personal preference and budget. For example, you could go for a plexiglass side table if you’re on a tight budget. But if you want something that’s more durable, then choose one made of Lucite.

Functionality

When you buy a side table, you should think about how you’ll be using it. For example, if you’re looking to put it next to the sofa, then look for an acrylic side table with shelf compartments. This way, you can store things like coasters, magazines, or TV remote controls.

But if you simply want to use it as a decorative piece, then you can go for a clear acrylic side table with no shelves. You can use it to display things, like vases, candles, or photo frames.

Cost

Last but not least, you need to consider your budget. Acrylic side tables can range in price, from a few hundred dollars to a few thousand. So, set a budget and stick to it.

You can find some great deals online or at garage sales. But if you want something that’s high quality and will last for years, then you might have to splurge a little bit.

Keep these factors in mind, and you’re sure to find the perfect clear acrylic side table for your needs!

Acrylic Side Table FAQs

Q: How do I take care of my clear acrylic side table?

A: You should dust it regularly with a soft, dry cloth. You can also use a mild cleaner or glass cleaner to remove any stubborn dirt or grime. Just be sure to avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners as they can damage the surface of the table.

Q: Can I put a clear acrylic side table outdoors?

A: Yes, you can! However, you need to make sure that it’s in a covered area so that it’s protected from the elements.

Q: How do I store clear acrylic furniture?

A: When not in use, clear acrylic furniture should be stored in a cool, dry place. You can also wrap it in bubble wrap or a soft cloth to prevent it from getting scratched.

Q: Can I repair any scratch or crack on my clear acrylic side table?

A: Yes, you can! You can use a clear acrylic repair kit to fix any minor damage. However, if the damage is severe, then you might need to replace the entire table.
