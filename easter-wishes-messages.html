Now that the Lenten season is coming to a close, it's time to turn our focus to Easter Sunday. It's a wonderful time of year, with warmer weather, Easter flowers, and special Easter traditions with your family. While it's easy to get caught up in the Easter egg hunts and baskets full of goodies and chocolate Easter bunnies, we could all use a reminder to reflect on Easter Bible verses and think about why we're celebrating in the first place. And then, it's time to share those special greetings and wishes with our friends and family and even colleagues.

Whether you want to send an Easter card or share a message on social media, it can be hard to find the best happy Easter wishes and greetings to share. Our list has messages for everyone, whether you're looking for an Easter quote or a religious Easter Bible verse or the best Easter prayers. For some on your list, there's nothing better than a funny Easter joke that can be used as a cute Easter Instagram captions. Regardless of the type of message you're looking for, there's a special greeting on this list just for you.
