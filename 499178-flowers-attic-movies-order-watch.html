However, even though such a long time has elapsed, the story still follows the twisted plight of the Dollanganger family.

The family attempts to leave their past behind but soon discovers that some secrets cannot be left behind. In a different twist, Cathy finds herself in an abusive relationship with Julian, a fellow dancer. To escape the abuse, she is forced to move in with Christopher, thus facing the forbidden feelings they developed during captivity.
