Best Woody Amber Scent

Baccarat Rouge 540 Eau de Parfum

Pros
  • Best-Selling

When you think of Maison Francis Kurkdjian, Baccarat Rouge 540 is likely the first scent that comes to mind. This sophisticated floral, woody fragrance, first created as a limited edition, was so wildly popular that it became part of the Maison's permanent collection.

Best Musky Floral

724 Eau de Parfum

Best Musky Floral

724 Eau de Parfum

Pros
  • Can be worn daily

Launched in 2022, this fresh EDP is named 724 because it is meant to be worn seven days a week, 24 hours a day.

Customer Review:

"I purchased this fragrance when it was first released. I wore it a couple of times and thought it was “nice,” but as the weather warms up and I wear it a little more consistently, I’ve realized how beautiful this fragrance truly is. The blend of the notes is masterful and makes it a new favorite of mine." —AngeeBee

Size6.8 oz
NotesWhite sandalwood, Aldehydes, Bergamot from Calabria, Jasmine from Egypt, Musk

Best Floral Chypre

Feminin Pluriel Eau De Parfum

Best Floral Chypre

Feminin Pluriel Eau De Parfum

  • Great for floral lovers
