When rumours of Taylor Swift and Matty Healey's romance first swirled in past weeks, fans of both appeared equally shocked. 

Outrage, elation and confusion amassed as people dug for hints that the Fearless hitmaker, 33, was dating the The 1975 frontman, 34, less than a month after her split from her boyfriend of six years, Joe Alwyn, became public.

Two weeks ago the pair were pictured together for the first time as they arrived at Taylor's Condo in Nashville, Tennessee - following her Eras country in the city - just after midnight. 

But as drama and mystery brews surrounding the elusive alleged romance, one must ask - do Sparks Fly between the pair astrologically?

Michelle Bell - founder of Cosmic Fusion - told FEMAIL that no matter what the general consensus on the couple may be, their 'vibrant and outgoing personalities complement each other perfectly'.

Here, she explains how their excitement and 'insatiable thirst for life' makes them a potential power couple in progress...

TAYLOR SWIFT: SAGITTARIUS EARTH SNAKE 

Fans of Taylor won't be surprised to hear that her Sagittarius Earth Snake makes for a 'classy, elegant, sassy and determined character'.

'Sagittarians are ordinarily reckless, mischievous, naughty, playful characters who bore easily and constantly seek new experiences and adventure,' Michelle wrote.

Certainly, the star has been candid in her lyrics about seeking out adventures and excitement in her romances.

'However, the Earth element and Snake are refined, elegant, dignified and sophisticated,' the astrology expert explained. 'It's an interesting combination of traits.'

'It's quite a lovely mix though as the Sagittarius imbues this character with some impulsive and exciting energy. They tend to have great taste and enjoy the finer things in life (Earth/Snake), they're also highly intuitive and enjoy the mysterious charm they exude.'

MATTY HEALY: ARIES EARTH SNAKE 

While opinion on Matty - whose past is riddled with controversies - is divided, many have commented on his undeniably engaging aura.  

'The Aries Earth Snake is a real charmer,' Michelle explains. 'A very relaxed character who is in complete control at all times.

'With the Earth element as their foundation, they're stable, reliable, and have a strong sense of practicality in their approach to life.

'They are intriguing and grounded individuals.'

While Earth Snakes can be 'just a little too laid back', this is cancelled out with Matty's 'fiery and action-packed' Aries.

But this isn't to be mistaken for volatility, Michelle warned.

'They're not your typical aggressive Aries though,' she said. 'They're pretty chilled on the whole. However, don't be misled by their laid-back, relaxed veneer. 

'They are powerful characters with real inner strength, it's just very well concealed. While they may appear reserved on the outside, they possess a hidden strength and determination.

'They are not easily swayed by external influences and prefer to make decisions based on their own judgement and analysis.'

Matty, who according to Rolling Stone told a recent concert audience that he 'feels bad' for having been 'a bit irresponsible' when it comes to what he jokes about, is 'highly intuitive and tend to act on their gut instincts'.

However, this also makes him a good empath. 

'They possess a calm and composed demeanour that instils a sense of trust and reliability in others,' Michelle explained. 'People are instantly drawn to them, they're fantastic listeners.

'When they put their minds to something, nothing is beyond them.'

ARE THEY COMPATIBLE?  

This couple and their characters are 'really compatible', the astrology pro ruled.

'Firstly, their Western signs (Sagittarius-Aries) are basically perfect,' she explains.

'Both Sagittarius and Aries thrive on excitement, freedom, and pushing boundaries. They both crave new experiences and have an insatiable thirst for life.

'Their vibrant and outgoing personalities complement each other perfectly.'
