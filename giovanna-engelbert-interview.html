Giovanna Engelbert thinks we should all be having more fun with jewellery

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  740 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

“What I am really trying to do at Swarovski is bring out what’s already in the brand,” Giovanna Engelbert tells me as we chat over Zoom, three years into her job as the first ever creative director of the famed Austrian jewellery brand. “It has been phenomenal to be part of a company with such a long history and this incredible heritage. That is what really sits at the core of the brand.”

Engelbert first joined in 2020 – it was the height of the pandemic and she didn’t meet any of her team in person for over a year, she says. Tasked with overseeing the creative vision of the company, she has unveiled a fresh look for the jewellery house; one which stresses the craftsmanship and beauty of the unique crystals it creates, but which also emphasises fun and creativity in fashion.

“I am a big supporter of having fun with jewellery,” she says. “That’s why Swarovski has been such a great fit for me. One of the amazing things about crystals, as opposed to fine jewellery, is that these pieces are available on a much larger scale for consumers. It doesn’t require quite the same gravitas, so we have more creative possibilities – we can have more fun with what we do.”

Courtesy of Swarovski

"I am a big supporter of having fun with jewellery"

This vision is very clear to see in Swarovski’s latest launch, a sunglasses collection that has been created in collaboration with EssilorLuxottica (an eyewear label Engelbert describes as “the best in class”). In the accompanying campaign – which was worked on by the legendary make-up artist Pat McGrath and shot by the illustrious Steven Meisel – the styling chimes with what Engelbert is saying. The model, Georgia Palmer, is dripping in crystals, wearing numerous layered statement necklaces, lots of earrings and a pair of sunglasses from the new collection, which perfectly match her jewels. More is definitely more.

Courtesy of Swarovski

“The jewellery very much informs the sunglasses,” Engelbert says of the inspiration behind these designs. “They are an extension of what we already have – it’s an expansion of that same self-expression.” Each pair of sunglasses has been created based on a specific jewellery collection, and statement crystals adorn every design in clever ways. None of them are subtle, and it's all about having a little fun with what you wear. Of course, that’s exactly what Engelbert has always been known for.

As one of the most high-profile fashion editors, Engelbert's personal style has been closely watched and admired for years. She remains one of the most beloved street-style stars, known for her love of high-octane glamour. How did she develop such a individual sense of style?

Edward Berthelot//Getty Images

“There are no rules,” she says. “Not everything works for everybody and there is no exact formula for great personal style. It is just about working out what feels good for you. What is going to make you the best version of yourself? That is what great style is all about. It’s not about having the most expensive possessions, it’s about an attitude. Fashion is nothing without people – a dress without a woman is nothing, it is the woman who brings life to the dress.”

"Great style is not about having the most expensive possessions, it’s about an attitude"

She adds that she loves to “mix casual and glamour together” and is always open to trying new things, but never wants to be dictated to by fashion. “The wheel spins every season, and there is always a new way of refreshing your look. Trends are incredibly fun so I would never say that I am against trends, but what I am against is becoming a victim of trends and jumping into something just because everyone else is wearing it. It's about finding out what suits you, and letting that evolve with you as you grow older.”

It is also all about authenticity, Engelbert says – and that's not true just of fashion, but of finding success in everything that you do. Find out what feels like you and you will look great; find out what you are passionate about and you will succeed. “I believe very strongly in authenticity. If you are doing what is right for you, then it will work out.”

The Swarovski x EssilorLuxottica sunglasses collection launches tomorrow, Tuesday 5 September.
