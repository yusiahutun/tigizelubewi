Taylor Cole Gets Married -- See Which &quot;Laguna Beach&quot; Stars Served as Bridesmaids!

 Posted on June 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  216 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Here comes the bride!

"Laguna Beach" star Taylor Cole tied the knot over the weekend and had a mini-reunion with some of her former cast mates.

Alex Murrel ("Alex M.") and Morgan Souders, who also appeared on season two of the MTV reality show, served as bridesmaids in the wedding.

Murrel took to Instagram to share a snap of her BFF looking gorgeous as she headed down the aisle with her new hubby Kevin Simshauser.

"My best friend is now a Simshauser @taylorcolephoto #palmsimswedding," she captioned the sweet snap.

In the pic, we get a look at the star's vintage-inspired beaded gown as she makes her way down the aisle with the real estate investor after saying "I Do".

Alex posted another picture from the reception, showcasing the bridesmaids' floral gowns and matching flower crowns.

"Had the best time celebrating my best friend @taylorcolephoto and her new husband @kevsims13 this weekend #palmsimswedding," she wrote.

The ladies have stayed close since appearing on the show back in 2005. Last year, Taylor served as a bridesmaid in Alex's wedding to Kyle Mark Johnson.
