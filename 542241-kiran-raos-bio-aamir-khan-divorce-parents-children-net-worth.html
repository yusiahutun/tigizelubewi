Early life

The producer was born on 7 November 1973 in Hyderabad, India. She was born into a Telugu-Bengali family of J. C. Rao, the son of Janumpally Rameshwar Rao, the former Raja of Wanaparthy state. Thus, Kiran Rao's parents come from royalty.

Although the personality was born in Hyderabad, she grew up in Kolkata. She attended Loreto House School in Kolkata, India. However, Kiran Rao's family left Calcutta, forcing her to move to Mumbai when she was 19.

She attended the Sophia College for Women and graduated in 1995 with a major in food sciences. After that, Rao enrolled for a Social Communications Media Course at the Sophia Polytechnic. Unfortunately, she quit the course after two months and left for Delhi.

Years later, Kiran graduated with a Master's at the AJK Mass Communication Research Centre at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi.

Professional career

The personality started her professional career in the film industry. She was an assistant director in the epic film Lagaan, directed by Ashutosh Gowariker. Ashutosh Gowariker also worked with Kiran several years later on the film Swades: We, the People.
