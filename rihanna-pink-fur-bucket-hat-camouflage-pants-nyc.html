Rihanna Pairs a Pink Fur Bucket Hat with Camouflage Pants in NYC

 Posted on February 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  483 words  |  Martina Birk

Rihanna is back at it with the bucket hats.

On Saturday night, the singer was spotted in New York City wearing a pink furry bucket hat with a matching camisole and camouflage pants by The Attico. She wore her hair in loose body waves and accessorized with layered necklaces, large thin gold hoop earrings, and white pointed-toe heels.

The weekend's look mirrors one from June, when Rihanna went on a date with boyfriend A$AP Rocky in a bright-pink fuzzy hat by Emma Brewin and a vintage pink slipdress by John Galliano for Dior. She additionally paired the look with strappy sandals from Tom Ford, a beaded necklace by Dior, and a complementary beaded shoulder bag by Fendi.

Rihanna has been seen out on the town a lot lately, most notably alongside Rocky, with the two making their romance official several months ago. The couple were seen filming a music video together in similar neon ensembles and were recently spotted recording together in the studio, presumably for Rocky's upcoming fourth album, All Smiles.

In June, Rihanna opened up to Interview magazine during a chat with actress Sarah Paulson about her own album plans, emphasizing that her production process has largely changed since balancing her makeup and lingerie lines, Fenty Beauty and Savage x Fenty. "It's the reason why an album isn't being spat out like it used to," she shared. "I used to be in the studio, only the studio, for three months straight, and an album would come out. Now, it's like a carousel. I do fashion one day, lingerie the next, beauty the next, then music the next. It's like having a bunch of kids and you need to take care of them all."

She guaranteed that a "really fun" album is still indeed in the works and that she's been working hard to put it out as soon as possible. "I'm really happy with a lot of the material we have so far, but I am not going to put it out until it's complete. It makes no sense to rush it, but I want it out. I've gotten to the point where I'm like, 'Even if I don't have the time to shoot videos, I'm going to put an album out.'" She added, "I wish I knew [how much longer fans will have to wait]. I have blocked off a solid period of time for the studio next month."
