Cat-Nap Corner

Jenna Sue Design

This stylish seating area doubles as a daybed for afternoon snoozes or overnight guests. As a bonus, built-in drawers add storage space for spare blankets and pillows. (Look closely and you’ll notice it’s kitty-approved, too.)

See more at Jenna Sue Design.

2

Wake-Me-Up Nook

SWEETEN

If you like to begin the day with a warm cup of coffee, this cheery, bright breakfast nook complete with printed pillows and light woods is for you. All that’s missing are the bacon and eggs.

See more at Sweeten.

3

Creative Cranny

Nesting With Grace

This adorable alcove proves that no window is too small for a seat. There’s hidden storage beneath the bench seat, and from the macrame pillow to the white brick wall, mix-and-match textures add an extra-cozy feel.

See more at Nesting With Grace.

4

Stand-Alone Bench

Less-Than-Perfect Life of Bliss

Don’t have the space (or the carpentry skills) for a built-in window seat? A tufted bench framed by existing, extra-tall curtains is a simple solution—no hammer and nails required.

See more at Less-Than-Perfect Life of Bliss.

5

A Place For a Pup

Nick + Alicia

Consider this the prettiest “dog bed” you ever did see. Shades of creamy white and cool blue give the space a calming ambiance, inviting you to snuggle up with your latest read (and your four-legged friend).

See more at Nick + Alicia.

6

Radiator in Disguise

Pinteresting Plans

Blogger Rachel Moore of Pinteresting Plans used textured white pillow covers to camouflage unsightly radiators and offer a place to sit—with a built-in seat heater, to boot. A soft woven pillow is the finishing touch.

See more at Pinteresting Plans.

7

Built-In Bookcases

Nesting With Grace

Though this particular window seat was created for a soothing nursery, the built-in bookcases on either side make it a perfect fit for any literature fan, young or old. Plus, the bench seat lifts up to reveal more storage space.

See more at Nesting With Grace.

8

White Wonderland

Sarah Joy Blog

This minimalist, white-washed space features a shiplap wall, a comfy striped throw, and unique lace-up pillows. Best of all? The ability to fully take in the (stunning) view, perfect while sipping hot cocoa and listening to your favorite tunes.

See more at Sarah Joy Blog.

9

Farm-to-Table Seating

The Inspired Room

This kitchen feels like it’s been plucked straight out of a charming Hamptons farmhouse, complete with a plush window seat. A key touch: The rustic sign hanging overhead gives the room added personality.

See more at The Inspired Room.

10

A Minimalist Space

Aleksandra Zlatkovic//Getty Images

Outfitted in flax-linen pillows and harmonious shades of gray, this Scandinavian-inspired space is made for embracing hygge, albeit with a modern aesthetic. Use the window sill to display some cheery additions, like candles and flowers.
