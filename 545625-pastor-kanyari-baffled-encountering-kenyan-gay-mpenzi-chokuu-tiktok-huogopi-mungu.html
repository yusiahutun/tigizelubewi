  • Pastor Kanyari and Mpenzi Chokuu's TikTok live elicited mixed reactions among netizens
  • The two content creators left fans amused with their conversation as Chokuu opened up to the man of the cloth about his sexuality
  • Kanyari was baffled by the Kenyan Germany-based TikToker's confession and said he would quit TikTok
  • Kanyari claimed he had never encountered a gay man, vowing to pray for Chokuu to become a pastor

Pastor Kanyari has been trending on TikTok after he went live with Kenyan Germany-based TikToker, Sammy Owiya, aka Mpenzi Chokuu.

Mpenzi Chokuu opens up to PaStor Kanyari about his sexuality

"Are you a man or a woman? You look like a man and a woman. Nikikuchukua utakuja ukiwa mwanaume? Mungu wangu! Leo nimekutana na nani? Mimi nitatoka (Will you come as a man? Oh my God! Who have I met today? I will leave) TikTok Chokuu. Mimi nitahama (I'll leave)," Kanyari said.

The proud Kenyan gay talked about his sexuality, leaving Kanyari shocked.

"Mimi natumika pande zote mbili. Naitwa chapati pendua (I am versatile. I am called a chapati," he said.

Shocked Kanyari continued to interrogate Chokuu, as he insisted he would quit TikTok.

"Utawachia nani lion?? Kaa hapa pastor usitoke (You don't want lions? Don't quit TikTok)," Chokuu urged the preacher.

Kanyari responded:

"Lakini mambo mnaongea ni makubwa kuliko lions (But what you're talking about is bigger than lions)!"

The father of two was left mouth agape, and he asked Chokuu:

"Huogopi Mungu (Don't you fear God)? I came to TikTok to save people. I didn't come here to die."

Kanyari then prayed for the gay content creator, vowing to continue praying for him, so as to get saved.
