85 Best Instagram Captions and Quotes for Couples 2024

 Posted on September 13, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1262 words  |  Larita Shotwell

From cozy date nights throughout the cuffing season, to bloom-filled spring strolls, to summer evenings spent taking in every sunset together, there are no shortage of couples' photo ops year round. And, of course, friends and family want see updates from their favorite pair, but what do you say in the caption? Enter this collection of the best Instagram captions for couples and quotes for couples too.

Whether it’s a new relationship post, a clever caption from your beach trip together, or (surprise!) an engagement caption, your words are just as important as the carefully curated selection of photos you share.

From cute to funny, non-cheesy to unabashedly lovey-dovey, you’ll find the perfect caption or quote here to cover every kind of couple’s post. Take your pick from unique short captions, funny love quotes, pop culture references, romantic song lyrics, movie quotes, and one-liners. If it’s a long-term relationship, you might also find our collections of wedding captions and anniversary quotes helpful. And if you're ringing in 2024 together, you definitely will want to peruse our New Year captions for couples too.

All that's left to do is hit post and watch the likes and comments roll in!
