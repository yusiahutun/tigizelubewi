BBC EastEnders Danielle Harold goes braless in wide-open blazer for snap as fans swoon

Former EastEnders star Danielle Harold left fans picking their jaws up off the floor thanks to her latest sizzling snap.

The actress, best known for playing Lola Pearce, is never shy to share a stunning social media post and show off her jaw-dropping figure. And earlier this week Danielle was back it - and she soon left her fans wanting more.

Taking to her Instagram, the 31-year-old looked nothing short of sensational in a Halloween costume that went down a storm with her followers. Posing to the camera, Lola appeared to ditch the bra for her saucy outfit.

The soap star rocked bright purple eyeshadow, bleached green eyebrows and a red gloss swiped on her plump pout. She switched up her famous blonde locks too, rocking a lush green wig that matched her eyebrows.

Finishing off her Joker-inspired look, Danielle had temporary tattoos adorned all over her neck that read: “Ah Ha.” In the caption, she penned: “Happy Halloween."

As expected, Danielle’s rather revealing costume went down a treat with fans. In the comments section, they were quick to pile high the compliments.

One fan said: “How gorgeous!” Someone else added: “Wow! Just wow.” A third gushed: “My god, you’re fit”. While another quipped: “Absolutely stunning.”

It comes after Danielle and Jamie Borthwick have reignited romance rumours, thanks to a recent "date night" they shared. The duo, known for their roles as on-screen couple Lola Pearce and Jay Brown on the BBC show, showcased their close bond in an amusing Instagram Stories video.
