The Modern Icon: Gianvito Rossi's Portofino sandal

 Posted on January 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  547 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

When it comes to investment pieces, you usually can't beat the classics, those designs which have continued to dominate and impress for decades. But, sometimes a piece comes along which becomes an automatic heirloom. These are newer investment pieces that are destined to become future classics, which we celebrate in our franchise The Modern Icon. This time, we're looking at Gianvito Rossi's Portofino sandal.

A favourite among Hollywood's biggest names, the Portofino is both a capsule classic and a standout piece thanks to the designer's modern take on the strappy sandal, which features a circular buckle, a skinny strap on the ankle and a high back. Coming in various colours and fabrics (including glossy leather and suede), the sandal is often the perfect finishing touch to an evening look, which is why the likes of Nicole Kidman and Jennifer Lawrence have turned to it for their most glamorous moments.

This year, the Portofino is celebrating its 10th birthday – and the brand is marking the occasion with a special capsule collection filled with metallic styles of the sandal, and is opening up about how the cult shoe came to be.

The story behind the sandal

Back in 2014, Rossi wanted to reinterpret the simple strappy sandal by restructuring the front upper and the ankle strap with modern proportions. This resulted in a minimal finish that would transcend the seasons, but still had a touch of drama about it.

Courtesy of Gianvito Rossi

"The Portofino sandal embodies the essence of my vision: beauty in simplicity and quality, as well as in the attention to proportions," explains Rossi. "Its design is conceived to enhance a woman's silhouette rather than prevail over her. After 10 years, our customers keep looking for it: I like to think that its lines have somehow got out of time to stay, becoming a cherished object women can always have within reach in their wardrobe."

The open-toed stiletto which features a simple thin strap over the toes and around the ankle only, but it has a high back to add to the dramatic effect to the ankle strap. Meanwhile, the buckle is covered in nappa leather, avoiding metal hardware in the clean lines, making it feel like a modern evening shoe that is versatile enough to be worn again and again.

Getty Images

Who has worn them?

Being so minimal and elegant, the Portofino is a very red carpet-appropriate shoe so there is no wonder that so many A-listers have stepped out in them.

Nicole Kidman wore a silver pair to the Cannes Film Festival in 2017, which proved the perfect accessory to her fringed Michael Kors' gown. Meanwhile, Diane Kruger wore them in Cannes in 2015, pairing them with a Chanel mini dress. Kim Kardashian, Kendall Jenner and Jennifer Lawrence have also been seen wearing them on occasion.

Victor Boyko

Where to shop

The Portofino is available to buy directly from the brand as well as at various retailers, including Net-a-Porter and Mytheresa in the UK, coming in 70mm, 85mm, 105mm heights. Meanwhile, the new metallic capsule launches today and is inspired by the colours associated with the Italian city it is named after, including bright pink, blue, green and orange.
