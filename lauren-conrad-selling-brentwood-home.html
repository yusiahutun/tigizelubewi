Lauren Conrad is selling her Los Angeles home for 3.45million: see photos

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  166 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Lauren Conrad is making some changes to her impressive property portfolio after listing her estate in Brentwood, Los Angeles for sale. The former star of The Hills, who welcomed her baby boy Liam James Tell in July, has put the home on the market for $4.5million (around £3.45million) according to property website Trulia.

MORE: See other celebrity homes here

The beautiful home was built in 1959 and has six bedrooms and five bathrooms set on a 12,000-square-foot lot. It has impressive amenities too; set behind private gates, Lauren's home has a spacious back garden, a large chef's kitchen and living room with a traditional wood burning fireplace.

Lauren bought the home in 2013 for $3.7million (around £2.84million), so looks set to make a good profit on her purchase. The fashion designer also owns two properties in her hometown of Laguna Beach, California, and recently sold her mansion in Pacific Palisades, California for $4.4million (£3.38million).
