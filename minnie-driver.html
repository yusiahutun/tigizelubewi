Early Life And Education Of Minnie Driver

Her mother, Gaynor Churchward (née Millington) is a former model and fabric designer.

Minnie Driver's Awards And Honors

In 1995, she won Chicago Film Critics Association Award for Circle of Friends for Most Promising Actress in 1995.

She received the Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress and a Screen Actors Guild Award for her role as Skylar in the drama Good Will Hunting in 1997 alongside Matt Damon. She received the London Film Critics' Circle Award in Best Supporting Actress of the Year for the same movie.

In 2007, she was honored with  California Independent Film Festival Award, Phoenix Film Festival, and Tiburon International Film Festival Award for Take. In 2010, she was awarded the Genie Award for Best Performance by an Actress in a Supporting Role for Barney's Version.

Moreover, she was nominated for Emmy Award and Golden Globe for her performance in the TV series The Riches.
