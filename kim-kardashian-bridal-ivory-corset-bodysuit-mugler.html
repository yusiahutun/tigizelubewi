Kim Kardashian Is a Bridal Dream in an Ivory Corset Bodysuit

 Posted on May 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  291 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kim Kardashian looked like she was pulled straight out of an Elizabethan oil painting in her latest photo dump.

Today the reality star shared behind-the-scenes images from a recent fitting, where she wore a silky ivory corset bodysuit by Thierry Mugler, which featured a sculpted hourglass silhouette. The strapless piece had a pointy cupped bra that plunged down at the center, while the bodice cinched Kardashian at the waist with sharp rods so skintight, they could have doubled as built-in body armor.

At the hips, the bodysuit plunged out to the sides, creating a dramatic contrast with the teeny-tiny waist, and transformed into boxy hot shorts.

The bridal piece seemed to take inspiration from royal high fashion of the 16th through 18th centuries, though Mugler—a champion of the highbrow naked look—added a modern edge with the daring neckline and pronounced waist-to-hip ratio.

Mugler’s affinity for body-shaping silhouettes feels right at home on Kardashian, who is in the body-shaping business herself, with her inclusive, much-coveted shapewear brand Skims.

Kardashian has supported Mugler for years, famously wearing a drippy, crystal-drenched bronze dress of his to the 2019 Met Gala (with then-husband Kanye West), where she paired it with a sexy wet-hair look people everywhere have been attempting to re-create ever since. She also wore a jaw-dropping Mugler design to the fifth annual Hollywood Beauty Awards that year: a skintight, underboob-baring LBD.
