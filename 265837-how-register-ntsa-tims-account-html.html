How to register for NTSA TIMS account: step-by-step guide

 Posted on October 5, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1098 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The National Transport and Safety Authority (NTSA) was set up to harmonise operations in various road transport departments. It assists in managing the road transport sub-sector in Kenya. The authority offers multiple services to citizens. Learning how to register a TIMS account is necessary if you own a vehicle or want a driving license.

The mission of NTSA is to enhance road safety for all road users. It offers services related to roads. To access these services, you need a Transport Information Management System (TIMS) account. If you wish to obtain a driving license or check your vehicle registration details, you must learn how to register a TIMS account.

How to register a TIMS account

The Government of Kenya has embraced the use of technology. Most services are now accessible online. This move has reduced the hustle of making very long queues to receive government services.

Requirements for a TIMS account registration

Before you start the registration process, ensure you have the following.

  • Your national ID (Kenyan or Alien)
  • KRA PIN
  • Mobile phone with a valid SIM card

How to set up a TIMS account: step-by-step guide

Follow the steps below to register for an NTSA TIMS account

  • Choose the category you would like to open your account. The available options are individual, dealer, agent, financial institution, company, or driving school.
  • Enter your ID Serial Number for individuals or KRA PIN for all other categories and click Verify.

  • The next section is the information section. For an individual account, some of your data will be filled automatically. Provide your mother's maiden name, KRA PIN, occupation, and mobile number.
  • Click Send Authorisation Code to receive a code on your phone.
  • Enter the code and click Verify.
  • More fields will appear on the screen. Fill them out and verify the information you have provided.
  • Click Next to complete your TIMS account sign up.
  • The NTSA TIMS SMS service will then send you a password that you will use to log in to your account. Note down your TIMS login details because you will need them whenever you wish to access your account.

How to log into your new NTSA TIMS account

Follow the steps below to learn how to log into your new NTSA TIMS account. Ensure you have your phone with you because you will need it to access the security code for logging in.

  • Visit the NTSA TIMS portal on an internet-enabled device.
  • Enter your ID number, username, or certificate of incorporation number and password.
  • Click Get Code to receive a one-time security code on your mobile phone.
  • Once you receive the code, type it in the box, then click Sign In.
  • Once logged into your account, change your password by clicking on your name at the top right corner of the page.
  • A new page will be opened where you will create your password.

Services offered on the NTSA TIMS portal

The actions you can complete on the portal are listed below.

  • View all vehicles you own
  • Apply for vehicle registration
  • Apply for transfer of your vehicle ownership to another party
  • Confirm ownership transfer
  • Register any physical or functional changes to your vehicles
  • Accept ownership for vehicles transferred to you
  • Apply for duplicate logbooks
  • Apply for reflective number plates
  • Book vehicle inspection
  • Apply for a new driving license/ driving license renewal
  • Driving test booking

How to recover your NTSA TIMS account

If you forgot your password and cannot access your account anymore, fret not. Follow the steps below to recover your account.

  • Visit the NTSA TIMS portal on an internet-enabled device.
  • Click Forgot Password.
  • On the password recovery page, provide your Username or ID number, and from the drop-down menu, select the category from which you had your account.
  • Choose the authorisation method you prefer.
  • Click Get Verification Code. A code will be sent to your phone via the NTSA TIMS SMS service.
  • Enter the code.
  • Select the security question you had set up during registration and provide the right answer.
  • Click Submit to move to the next step, where you will create a new password and confirm it.
  • Click Submit to complete the account recovery process.

What are the benefits of using the NTSA TIMS portal?

The portal saves time and is a secure way of accessing various NTSA services. Unlike before, you can access multiple services within a few minutes.

Why does the NTSA TIMS portal say person not found when applying?

The message above pops up because you may have used a serial number from a lost national ID that you have since recovered. Instead, use the serial number of the new national ID you got (the replacement).

Does NTSA have an app?

The NTSA has an app you can download from your Google or App store. Download and install the app to access various services.

How do I activate my NTSA TIMS account?

Once you register, log into the account and reset the password the first time you log in. This will automatically activate your account.

How do I check my motor vehicle records?

You can check your records by logging into your TIMS account. Once you are logged in, you will be able to view all your details.
