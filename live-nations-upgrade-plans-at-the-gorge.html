Live Nation's Upgrade Plans At The Gorge

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  99 words  |  Larita Shotwell

EPHRATA (CelebrityAccess) – Plans filed Grant County Planning Department reveals some of Live Nation's plans for upgrades at the Gorge Amphitheatre.

The plans, filed with Grant County Planning Department Director Damien Hooper said that Live Nation seeks to remodel several buildings, including the the cliff house hospitality tent, the hilltop food concession stands and the convenience store, iFiber News reported.

Live Nation also plans to construct 150 cottages at the amphitheatre campground, as well as adding a wastewater lagoon near the camping facilities.

The plans, if approved, are the first phase of a five-year project. – Staff Writers
