Warning: This is most definitely NSFW.

Miley Cyrus is not shy about trumpeting her man's ... umm ... game in the bedroom.

The pop star alluded to her longtime boyfriend Liam Hemsworth's prowess in the sliest and most obvious of Internet humble brags -- a celeb comment on a viral meme.

On Monday night, the "Nothing Breaks Like the Heart" singer responded to a viral meme posted from the Instagram account Sour Psycho that suggested no man has all five of these qualities: "good dick game, empathy, a height above 5'9, no hoes and common sense."

"Mine does! Don't give up!" Miley commented on the meme, as pointed out by Comments by Celebs.

The popular Instagram account, that is loved by celebrities and fans alike, wrote in the caption of the screenshot, "As we suspected, @liamhemsworth." We can't say we're surprised either.

Fans commented on Miley's response to the Instagram photo, writing that the actress gave them "hope" to find a significant other that's just as amazing as Hemsworth. Another person wrote, "Marry him sis."

However, it seems that Miley isn't the only star that has nailed a beau with the whole package. "Riverdale" star Lili Reinhart, who's in a relationship with her co-star Cole Sprouse, also commented on the post, writing "I found one!"

If you didn't know, Cole's twin brother, Dylan, is currently dating Victoria Secret model Barbara Palvin. This prompted Comments by Celebs to assume that since Cole has the five qualities then Palvin must have had the same experience as Reinhart.
