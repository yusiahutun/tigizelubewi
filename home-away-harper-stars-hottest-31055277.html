Home and Away fans have just been introduced to Summer Bay's newest resident Harper Matheson, but actress Jessica Redmayne lives a much more glamorous life away from the soap

Home and Away fans were introduced to Harper Matheson in recent scenes.

During Wednesday's instalment (September 27) of the Channel 5 soap, social worker Harper arrived in Summer Bay looking for a place to stay and came across Irene's (Lynne McGranger) room for rent.

Despite John's (Shane Withington) warning that Harper is not to be trusted, the newcomer moved into Irene's and the two have become close friends.
