Adele stuns fan by responding to his social media message

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  562 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Her career has soared since first hitting the music scene in 2006.

And Adele left one of her fans starstruck on Saturday after she replied to his social media message and said that she is 'chuffed' that he loves her music.     

The Hello hitmaker, 32, left Colyn, who runs an Adele fan account, stunned as he admitted he was 'bawling his eyes out' over the response.
