While strolling the web, we came by another handsome young actor with a mysterious personal life. Today, we will be discussing about the personal life of Iain De Caestecker, who is best known for his role of Agent Leo Fitz in the television series called Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

Talking about Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D,  we have covered the personal life of another fellow S.H.I.E.L.D agent, Nick Blood, who was casual about letting everybody know about his girlfriend, unlike Iain who ha maintained privacy when it comes to his personal life.

Sadly, Iain De Caestecker is not like his friend Nick Blood. Iain has decided to keep his personal life private, and we must say that he has successfully done it.

Is he Dating Someone? Married?

Iain is currently 28 years old and unmarried. 

Because there is no information about his past and current relationships on Tabloids, people tend to label him gay.

Iain De Caestecker’s Short Bio:

Caption: Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D: Elizabeth Henstridge and Iain De Caestecker Exclusive Interview(2013)

Iain De Caestecker was born on December 29, 1987 (age 28) in Glasgow, Scotland as one of the four children, Iain has a twin sister. Iain studied drama at the Langside College. He started his acting career in early 2000 when he got a role in "The Little Vampire", a comedy horror.

When I was younger, me and my brother got a video camera, and he used to direct and I used to act. We used to make these silly, stupid short films, which, looking back now, were probably horrible. [laughs] I remember at the time we thought it was the best things ever.

Iain on his early film experience, in an interview with Discovery channel in 2011

His notable movies include 16 Years of Alcohol (2000), Shell (2012) and Lost River (2014). His notable television credentials include Coronation Street (2001-2003), The Fades (2011), and much more.

 He was awarded SET Award in 2014 and TVLine’s Performer of the week in 2015.  He is currently working on Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D (since 2013) alongside Henry Simmons, Brett Dalton, and Nick Blood. This year, he was also seen in" Ultimate Spider-Man."
