Is She Expecting Timothee Chalamets Baby?

 Posted on June 20, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1061 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

With rumors swirling that she’s been hiding some exciting news, fans are pouring over Kylie Jenner’s pregnancy photos from the past to decipher if she’s expecting a baby with her boyfriend Timothee Chalamet. The A-listers were first linked in April 2023 when DeuxMoi posted an anonymous tip that claimed “multiple sources have told me that Timmy C has a new girl…Kylie Jenner. ⚰️.”

On April 2, 2024, podcaster Daniel Tosh made totally unfounded claims that the Kardashians star was expecting a baby with her boyfriend of 12 months. “Here’s something crazy. I went to the grocery store in Malibu, and I was talking to an employee there. I try to avoid it but I was talking to this guy, and I said, ‘Why were you guys closed yesterday?’ He goes, ‘Well, I’m not supposed to discuss it, but Keeping Up With the Kardashians, the show, was filming their season finale here yesterday,’” Tosh claimed.

“Spoiler alert, this is for the upcoming season of the show. This is the big season finale. This random grocery store person is telling me that they rented out the entire store, shut it down and then, they acted like they were grocery shopping,” He added: “This was the scene that apparently happened: Kylie reveals that she’s pregnant again with Timothée’s kid. What a bombshell.” Tosh added, “This might not be true. I am not verifying anything. This is what an employee at a grocery store in Malibu told me.”

Kylie Jenner’s pregnancy photos

Now, fans are growing convinced that the Keeping Up With The Kardashians star is expecting once more. Here are Kylie Jenner’s pregnancy photos and how she managed to keep her first child completely under wraps.

  • July 2017: 2 months (ish) pregnant with Stormi

    This photo of Kylie Jenner was taken in July 2017. The most likely conception date for a baby due on February 1, 2018, was May 10, 2017, based on a “typical” 40-week pregnancy, so at this point, Kylie would be two months into her pregnancy and unlikely to be showing much at this point.

    As to why she kept her pregnancy hidden until after Stormi’s birth, she wrote on Instagram: “There was no gotcha moment, no big paid reveal I had planned. I knew my baby would feel every stress and every emotion, so I chose to do it this way for my little life and our happiness. Pregnancy has been the most beautiful, empowering and life-changing experience I’ve had in my entire life, and I’m actually going to miss it. I appreciate my friends and especially my family for helping me make this special moment as private as we could.”

  • July 2017: 2 months (ish) pregnant at Wireless Festival

    Again, this picture taken at Wireless Festival as she watches Travis Scott perform would’ve been one month (approximately) or less into her pregnancy with Stormi, so it’s unlikely she’d be showing any bump. The pregnancy “pop” usually happens around 16 weeks to 20 weeks.

  • May 2021: The month Aire was likely conceived

    Kylie may not have even known she was pregnant at the time this photo was taken on May 22, 2021, so there’s nothing to hide at this point.

  • June 2021: 2 months-ish pregnant with Aire

    Once again, the likely conception date for her son, Aire (formerly Wolf) was around May 10 2021 so Kylie would be around two months pregnant at the time this photo was taken on June 15, 2021.

  • September 2021: Big accessories

    While out for lunch on New York’s Upper East Side, Kylie used two very loud accessories to draw attention away from her pregnant belly. The cow print boots and the oversized faux fur hat ensure that eyes are basically looking everywhere but her midsection. This photo was taken on September 10, 2021, three days after she announced she was pregnant on Instagram, however, so it’s not like she was deliberately hiding anything.

  • September 2021: All white

    Wearing white might seem counter-intuitive for someone trying to hide a pregnancy belly but for someone who gets photographed everywhere she goes, it actually makes total sense. The flashes from the paparazzi actually flatten out curves and creases because the light bounces off the fabric. Again, this photo was taken after Kylie made her announcement on Instagram.

  • September 2021: Revolve NYFW

    Bright colors are a surefire way to hide a pregnancy belly, as are oversized fits. Jenner ticks both boxes in this orange look for Revolve’s NYFW presentation. This photo was taken on September 9, 2021, after she announced her pregnancy.

  • March 2024: Vanity Fair Oscars Party

    Taken on March 10, 2024, three weeks before the pregnancy rumors began.

  • Late March 2024: Black latex

    This photo was taken on March 21, 2024—Daniel Tosh claimed that Kylie Jenner was pregnant on April 2. In response to the rumor, sources close to the Keeping Up With The Kardashians production team told Us Weekly they didn’t film their season finale—or any scenes—this season in the grocery store. 

    It’s pretty clear from this photo that Jenner is not pregnant despite what fans so desperately want to believe because there’s no way in hell a woman trying to hide a pregnancy would wear a skin-tight black latex dress.

    It’s not enough to convince fans though. On Reddit, one user observed that the reality TV star had been quieter than usual on social media, as well as sharing a lot of sponsored content rather than candids: “The sweats, going dark on Instagram once again, reposting old ads on Insta… Oh I’m onto her…” they noted. Another wrote: “Kylie not beating the pregnancy allegations…”

    It’s not to say she hasn’t been posting on the grid, but fans are discerning. Instagram photos can, of course, be taken and published at any time. “This is also why I’m entertaining the Kylie pregnancy rumours as possible fact… It’s super easy to take a bunch of pics in different outfits, with different hairstyles, in slightly different locations and save them to post for next year,” one wrote.

  • April 26, 2024: Kylie responds to pregnancy rumors

    On April 26, 2024, Kylie Jenner took to Instagram stories (which have since expired) to show off her new denim collection in her Khy by Kylie Jenner fashion line. Wouldn’t you know it, she was wearing a cropped top to display a flat, definitely-not-pregnant stomach. She didn’t address the rumors vocally, but you get the idea this was her subtly responding.
