Christina Milian engaged to music mogul boyfriend Jas Prince

 Posted on July 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  251 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Christina Milian is engaged to her long-time boyfriend Jas Prince, according to new reports.While the AM to PM singer has previously dodged rumours that the couple were planning to tie the knot, the happy news has now been allegedly confirmed.Christina and Jas have been engaged since May, according to People, with an insider saying that the CEO of Rap-A-Lot Records is "a great match for her"."Christina is over-the-moon happy," another source told Life & Style magazine. "She really truly enjoys their active relationship. They love to go fishing, out to nightclubs and just hang out at home. They simply enjoy each other's company."
