KDF recruitment 2021: requirements, dates, forms and centres

 Posted on April 9, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  820 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The ministry of internal security recently released the 2021 KDF recruitment details. All potential candidates were urged to send their applications before the deadline. Are you interested in joining the KDF? Are you familiar with the recruitment dates and other specifics, including physique and academic requirements?

Many Kenyans look forward to working for Kenya's government in various capacities. KDF placement is one of the occupations that encounter massive applicants. With most applicants being fresh high school graduates, competition for KDF jobs continues to be quite competitive in the country.

The Kenya Defence Force recruitment process

With a lot of corruption rumours tagged alongside securing Kenya Defence Forces jobs, the government, through the ministry of internal security, issued a clear communication as listed below:

  • Recruitment is free for all applicants
  • Bribery results in imprisonment
  • No one should fast-track the KDF shortlisting process
  • Any suspicious activity must be reported to the nearest police station or military camp

KWS recruitment requirements, application forms, and salaries

The 2021 KDF recruitment dates began from 12th February to 21st March. The process was undertaken every Monday to Friday before 6:00 pm.

Announcement of successful candidates will be through the local dailies and other print media. After that, successful candidates will be issued with calling letters from their respective recruitment centres.

How to apply for KDF recruitment

KDF recruitment applications are done annually. Potential interests must await the ministry of internal security to announce enlistments dates. Once the communication is sent out, interested candidates are expected to select their preferred centre and make applications.

KDF recruitment qualifications

Service delivery is based on the highest professional standards. With KDF's commitment to upholding integrity, the general requirements for the KDF selection criteria are as follows:

  • Be a Kenyan citizen with no dual citizenship
  • Minimum KCSE mean grade D (plain)
  • Have a valid Kenyan National Identity Card and Pin Certificate
  • Applicants must be between 18 to 26 years
  • Applicants must have a clean criminal record
  • Applicants must meet all the medical and fitness requirements for KDF recruitment.
  • The minimum height for male applicants is 1.60m (5ft 3in) and women 1.52m (5ft)
  • Minimum Weight for male candidates is 54.55 Kg (120 lb) and female counterparts 50 Kg (110 lb).
  • Female candidates must NOT be pregnant at the time of recruitment and during the entire training duration.

KDF recruitment requirements for various entry levels

Prerequisite recommendations vary depending on the Kenya Army recruitment entry-level. However, the general requirements still apply with the variance inclined towards academic qualifications. Below are some of the entry-level structures and their required selection criteria:

Technicians

Here are the requirements to become a technician in the KDF:

  • K.C.S.E minimum mean grade C (plain)
  • Applicants must have a diploma or higher diploma from a reputable institution
  • Applicants must be registered with the relevant statutory body where applicable

Artisans

Below are the laid out conditions for you to become an artisan in the KDF:

  • K.C.S.E minimum mean grade D (+)
  • Candidates must hold a relevant certificate with Government Trade Test Grade II.
  • Craft II certificate from a recognized institution is accepted for KDF recruitment at the Artisan entry-level.

Constables

To become a constable in the KDF applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Must be an ex-KDF service member or ex-NYS member
  • Aged between 30-35 years forex-KDFs and 35-45 years for ex-NYS
  • Hold a Good Conduct Certificate with a minimum score of "Very Good"
  • At least nine years working experience forex-KDF colour service
  • Must have no criminal record

There are several entry levels to the army forces, which require different qualifications. The three listed above are just a few. You can check out more categories from the Ministry of Defence website.

It should be noted that all KDF recruitment certificates must be presented in original for verification purposes. The Ministry of Defence Forces shall, after that, accept document copies for filing. Scanned ID cards and police abstracts are not accepted. Likewise, all applicants are expected to submit their interests before the respective deadline.
