Salma Hayek Shares Rare Wedding Photos for Anniversary

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  303 words  |  Martina Birk

Salma Hayek is feeling the love. The actress celebrated her 15th wedding anniversary to fashion billionaire François-Henri Pinault, and provided a sweet — and rare — look at their 2009 wedding via Instagram.

"There are no words to explain the blessing of finding your soulmate. For all of you who found it, never take it for granted," she captioned a series of carousel images. "For all of you who haven’t, never give up #tbt to one of the best days of my life."

The photos featured Hayek looking stunning in a bridal corset, a look at her two dresses for the evening, and a final image of her and Pinault preparing to exchange vows in front of their wedding guests.

Hayek and Pinault technically have two marriage anniversaries. The pair first tied the knot with an intimate courthouse ceremony on Valentine's Day in 2009, and later had their actual wedding in Venice by April of the same year.

In February, Hayek celebrated the milestone with another Instagram post, a simple black and white "After 18 years together and 15 years of marriage, you still make loving you easy, fun, deep and fresh like a gentle breeze. Happy anniversary mi amor," she captioned the Instagram post.

Hayek typically doesn't offer too many looks into her relationship with Pinault, but back in 2017 she gushed about choosing the right person to be with. "I married the right guy. That is probably the most important thing," she said at the time. "We support each other in everything we do. We want the other one to strive. Makes you happy when the other one strives. And you know what, we don't have a very strong social life, because we really like to spend time together. So we do spend a lot of quality time together."
