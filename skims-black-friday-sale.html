Best Skims Cyber Monday Sale 2023 Deals to Shop

Skims has proven it’s so much more than a brand co-founded by a famous face. (Kim Kardashian, as you probably know.) In a few short years, Skims has become a lingerie destination: the best bras for big boobs, small boobs, and every size in between are all found there. In Skims’ Cyber Monday 2023 deals, which went live on November 22 and are still going strong until midnight, these top-rated pieces and more are deeply discounted.

Here are the sale details we've all been waiting for: the Skims bi-annual sale covers most of the online store, with savings between 20 percent and 50 percent off. Prices begin at $8 for a butterfly satin thong and max out at $104 for a satin robe. The brand hasn't announced when the deals will disappear, but we can safely assume it will last through Cyber Monday (today, November 27).

There are an overwhelming number of items to choose from, inclusive of waist-cinching corsets, smoothing T-shirts, and the TikTok-famous slip dress. Don't overthink what to buy; we recommend starting with the basics. Bazaar editors all agree that Skims bralettes, underwear, and shapewear live up to the hype. Begin with the label’s “Fits Everybody” line for size-inclusive and comfortable lingerie. If you want something more sculpted, Skims’ bodysuits and shaping shorts are a favorite to wear under formal dresses and tighter silhouettes. Bazaar editors have also reviewed Skims swimwear with shop-worthy results.

Where hyped up brands like Skims are concerned, it’s best to bring in experts to help identify what to order. Sale items at Skims usually sell out quickly in most sizes. (I've been burned by waiting too long before—and I've covered the Skims Cyber Monday sale for three years now.) Below, I've curated the best Skims Cyber Monday 2023 deals to shop before they disappear. Word to the wise: If you miss the pieces you want at Skims, select items are also on sale at Net-a-Porter and Nordstrom.
