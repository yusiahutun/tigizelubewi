Early Life of Craig Federighi

Born in an American family, Craig Federighi found his passion in technology at an early age. His curiosity in technology held him to lead his career in the field of technology engineering. Though his passion for technology was seen while he was just ten years old when he had begun to experiment coding.

His strong interest in technology later led him to join Acalanes High School in California. There, he initiated to make an early replica “Video On Demand” services. Later, he went to study at the University of California and received a master of science degree in Computer Science. Prior to that, he had completed his bachelor’s graduating with Science in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

Career Highlights

As many of us already know about the founder of Apple Inc., Steve Jobs, it would not surprise you all that Craig Federighi received an opportunity to work under Steve for NeXT, Inc., a former American software company that was also founded by Steve Jobs. After Apple Company acquired NeXT, he worked for nearly three years (1996-99), and later went to another software information company, Ariba where he served as a Chief Technology Officer.

In 2009, Craig came back to Apple and started creating macOS engineering. After receiving the post of the senior vice president at Software Engineering Apple, he encompassed iOS to Mac Software Engineering. He is always eager to add more features on the products of Apple Inc.
