Jinger Duggar shares 'sweetest' moments between rarely-seen daughters

 Posted on April 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  428 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

'I love every part of being a mom,' said the new author

    Jinger Duggar has shared her love of being a mom, after telling fans how she planned to keep her two girls out of the spotlight.

    The star is mom to two young girls, Felicity, two, and six-month-old Evangeline Jo, and she has now opened up on watching their relationship "blossom".

    "I love every part of being a mom and can’t imagine my life without my two little girls," Jinger told

    "I love seeing Felicity at this stage, how she adores her little sister. It really is the sweetest thing watching their relationship blossom. Every time Baby Jo cries, Felicity wants to do absolutely anything she can to make her little sister happy."
