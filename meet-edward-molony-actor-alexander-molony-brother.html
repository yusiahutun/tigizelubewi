Meet Edward Molony, Peter Pan & Wendy Actor Alexander Molony Brother

Edward Molony is the younger brother of Peter Pan & Wendy actor Alexander Molony. Claire-Louise Molony has been listed as the mother and primary guardian by Fashionuer for the two brothers. Alexander was born and reared in London; however, the identity of his father is not currently known to the general world.

According to reports, Claire-Louise manages her son’s open Instagram account in addition to serving as a specialist for Flight Centre Travel Group. In 2021, Alexander shared a few pictures of himself and his father enjoying the outdoors while writing about a trip they had taken to Lynn Canyon Park on social media. Alexander’s parents are obviously concerned for his and Edward’s happiness and are supportive of their acting pursuits, despite the fact that nothing is known about them.

According to Alexander’s IMDB bio, Edward was inspired to start acting because of Edward. Alexander’s debut feature, Peter Pan & Wendy, and undoubtedly not his last. Maybe he and Edward will work together on a big-screen project someday.

On National Siblings Day 2021, Alexander posted on his IG, “Happy #nationalsiblingsday day to my slightly crazy younger brother. We might sometimes annoy each other, but you’re my best friend and I miss the fun times we have together. Can’t wait until we can have more adventures.”

Peter Pan & Wendy, a streaming exclusive on Disney Plus, stars Alexander alongside Ever Anderson, Yara Shahidi, Jude Law, and more.

Alexander Molony turned 15 years old in September 2021 and is older than Edward Molony. This means, in September 2022, Alexander reached 16 years old. To support the release of the newest Marvel movie, Eternals, Alexander co-narrated a series of animated shorts for Marvel HQ’s Youtube channel titled The Untold Tales of the Eternals.

Alexander developed a passion for voice acting at the age of eight when he was required to do the voice-over for a commercial in which he was also acting. From there, he began to voice animated characters like Claude and Ooo Ooo in Raa Raa the Noisy Lion. From January to August of this year, he was in Canada filming the starring role of Peter Pan in Disney’s live-action version of J.M. Barrie’s beloved story, “Peter Pan and Wendy.”

Is Edward Molony An Actor?

Yes, Edward Molonu is an actor. Both Edward and Alexander Molony, who are in their first and fourth years, have been kept very busy with voice-over work. The second season of Ricky Zoom, which debuted this summer and is presently airing on Channel 5 and Nick Jr., has been Edward’s primary project.

When he was seven years old, he was chosen to play the role of Buster Bunker, the grandson of the police bike, in the first season of Ricky Zoom. He was requested to return for the second season.

The UK dub of a Lego Christmas TV advertisement was one of the ads that Edward voiced when he was old enough to join his brother in the voiceover business. In the E4/Netflix horror comedy Crazyhead, he was cast as Tom, the demon’s son. He also starred in a number of commercials, including Christmas ones for Quality Street, DFS, TK Maxx, and Tesco.

Edward was thrilled to be selected to be a Playground Pundit for One Day for Children at the 2019 Cricket World Cup, as he got to introduce the England and Indian captains to the press and interview other renowned cricketers. Edward also presented short film promotions for Shell at the E-Prix in Berlin and Cricket World Cup/Unicef “One Day for Children,” as he loves cricket.

According to his IMDB, he has appeared on Ricky Zoom, McDonald’s: Ralphie, Estranged, and Crazyhead. In the Hilda Tie-In Series audiobook, he provides the voice for the character Trevor.
