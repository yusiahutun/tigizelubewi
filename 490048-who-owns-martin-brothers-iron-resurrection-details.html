Martin Brothers is a custom vehicle building and fabrication company based in Texas Hill Country, USA. The company specializes in transforming automobiles in bad shape and turning them into appealing automobiles. It became prominent in 2016 when it was picked up for a reality show titled Iron Resurrection by Discovery Channel. Since then, most of its customers have been interested in knowing who owns the company. So, who owns Martin Brothers on Iron Resurrection?

Martin Brothers came into the spotlight after being featured on the famous reality show Iron Resurrection, which mainly revolves around Jason Martin. It traces his journey as he restores cars and motorcycles destined to be destroyed. Some other cast members include Pompa and Manny.

Who are the owners of Martin Brothers Customs in Texas?

While it may be called Martin Brothers because of Joe and Jason Martin, the company is owned and operated by award-winning custom car and motorcycle builder Joe Martin, who oversees every aspect of each build, from concept to creation. Joe works as the lead mechanic and artist for the company and uses old boats and vintage materials to make bikes.
