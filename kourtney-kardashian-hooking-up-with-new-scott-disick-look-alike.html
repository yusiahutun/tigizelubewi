Kourtney Kardashian Dating New Scott Disick Lookalike!

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  490 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Kourtney Kardashian may be over Scott Disick, but let’s just say the reality TV star definitely has a type!

And it’s got Scott’s features written all over it. 

The 39-year-old mother was spotted leaving Nobu in Malibu with the mysterious David Dee Duron on January 26, according to Radar. 

And rumor has it, they may actually be a couple! 

The pair was captured smiling and looking very friendly together while leaving the beach side restaurant, though Kourtney reportedly claims they are just friends.

But, come on, we know better! 

A friend close to the Kardashian told Radar that the two "did hook up" and she thinks he looks like a "hotter" and "younger" version of longtime boyfriend and baby daddy Scott Disick.

So, who even is this young man who has possibly stolen Kourtney’s heart?

Here are 5 things to know about her new boy toy:

1. Duron is a southern boy from Louisiana and has also had his moment in the spotlight as a former football player and model. 

2. He is now an accountant located in Miami.

3. Like the Kardashians, Duron has a big family, including four other brothers and sisters. 

4. He is a devoted Christian and the two actually met at Church.

5. Kris Jenner and the rest of the family already support him, and an insider told Radar that "he’s been vetted by the family."

While Kourtney lives in LA, she is not expecting the fling to go anywhere, though sources say Duron may be visiting Hollywood often. 

A source also added, "she does love her men young and he totally fits the bill!"

Kourtney’s latest men include Younes Bendjima, 25 and Luka Sabbat, 20. 

She dated Younes for more than a year and then jumped right into dating another model, one who isn’t even old enough to order a drink! 

Kourtney seems to be unfazed by the age difference, Hollywood Life claims. She doesn’t think guys her age "could handle her."

"Kourtney really doesn’t feel like she’s almost 40, she’s actually feeling younger than she has in years. She’s never felt sexier or more in control and dating younger guys just adds to her feelings of empowerment," a source tells Hollywood Life.

It does make us wonder if there is a little competition going on ever since Scott started dating 20-year-old Sofia Richie. 

But, an insider of the reality star adds, "Kourtney started dating Scott when she was still in her twenties. She doesn’t regret anything because she has her beautiful kids thanks to that relationship, but she does feel like she’s missed out on a lot."

It sounds like her friends and family approve. The close insider shared that "everyone thinks he’s a good guy for Kourtney. It’s in the early stages but they think highly of him." 

More power to you, Kourt! It’s all fun and games until you know, you fall in love.
