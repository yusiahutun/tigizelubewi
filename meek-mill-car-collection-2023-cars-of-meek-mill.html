Robert Rihmeek Williams (born May 6, 1987), known professionally as Meek Mill, is a popular American rapper. Meek started his career after signing with rapper T.I’s record label in 2008. Mill has released over 5 studio albums to date featuring some of the biggest artists in the world. Meek earns more than $2 million yearly and has a net worth of over $30 million. Let’s look at what cool car rapper Meek Mill has in his amazing car collection.

1. Rolls-Royce Ghost – $430,000

Starting with the mighty Rolls-Royce Ghost that rapper Meek Mill has got inside his expensive car collection. The Rolls-Royce Ghost features an extravagant exterior, and a super-rich cabin fitted with expensive elements. This Ghost is among the best expensive cars in the market for people with an appetite for high-end luxury and handcrafted design. The Ghost packs a super-refined turbocharged V12 engine that makes 563 hp of power. The Rolls’ provides one of the best driving experiences with supreme drive and plenty of power delivery.

2. Bentley Flying Spur – $270,000

Rapper Meek Mill has a luxurious Bentley Flying Spur in his expensive car collection. The Flying Spur features a premium-looking exterior blended with a high-end interior and a super-refined turbocharged engine. The Spur offers an extraordinary design handcrafted with precision and offers refined performance. The Flying Spur packs a powerful 6.0L twin-turbo W12 engine that shells out a hefty 626 hp of power. 5. Aston Martin Rapide – $210,000
