Dianna Agron has wardrobe malfunction at Alexander McQueen gala

 Posted on May 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  613 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

It was an evening celebrating the life and work of one of the greatest designers of our time, and Dianna Agron was no doubt thrilled to be at the prestigious and star-studded event.

But the Glee actress was probably not so happy when her beautiful dress revealed slightly too much as she later left the Alexander McQueen: Savage Beauty Fashion Gala, held on Thursday evening at London's V&A Museum.

The 28-year-old had wowed on the red carpet in her floor-length black gown by Johanna Ortiz with an incredibly deep plunging neckline and full skirt.

Whoops! Dianna Agron accidentally exposed too much of herself as she left the Alexander McQueen gala in London on Thursday night, the low-cut top in which she went braless slipping open

But the V-neck design of the garment betrayed her later on as she gave more than just a glimpse of her side-boob and cleavage while going braless.

With the well-cut dress hovering dangerously low and just above her navel, it's almost a wonder that she didn't fully expose herself throughout the evening.

Despite the wardrobe malfunction, though, the blonde beauty looked to be having a great time mingling with the likes of Kate Moss, David and Victoria Beckham, Naomi Campbell, Princess Beatrice and Poppy Delevingne.

Flawless: Despite her wardrobe malfunction, Dianna cut an impeccable figure at the Alexander McQueen: Savage Beauty Fashion Gala in her glorious gown at the V&A Museum

Punky details: The Glee star offset her elegant dress with a mussed-up hair style, smoky eyes and vintage necklaces

She looked impeccable on the red carpet in her black gown, with its flattering full skirt with a slight wrapover detail, and a pale blue satin ribbon tied around her waist.

The American star edged up her glamorous look with her blonde bob styled into a messy 'do, a pleasantly haphazard halo around her face while her eyes were dramatically enhanced with a sultry, smoky shadow.

She finished her showstopping look with a couple of decorative necklaces sat neatly on her decolletage, and a chic little clutch bag in charcoal grey.

Malfunction: However, as she turned to the side her breast was clearly visible

Flashing some skin: The US actress flaunted her legs as she stepped out after the gala, the wrapover skirt parting as she walked

As she stepped out later on, leaving the venue after enjoying the spoils of the evening, she revealed that the dress had a rather high split up the front, flaunting her slender legs as she walked.

Dianna has recently been spending time over in the UK and also in the French capital, where she attended a number of catwalk shows during Paris Fashion Week.

And she also enjoyed herself during awards season, attending the BAFTA Awards in February in London, and Harvey Weinstein's prestigious post-show bash.

She has also been busy promoting her new movie, political thriller Zipper, in which she stars opposite Patrick Wilson.

The movie follows married and rising prosecutor Sam Ellis (Patrick), who sets his sights on his office's beautiful intern, Dalia (Dianna).

After an inappropriate encounter with the looker, Sam decides to hire an escort to avoid an office romance with Dalia.
