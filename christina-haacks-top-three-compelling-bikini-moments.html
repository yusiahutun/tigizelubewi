Christina Haack's Top Three Compelling Bikini Moments

 Posted on June 17, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  796 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

She might not be a big fashion lover, but Christina Haack’s love of a bikini is unmatched. Here are three times the star stunned in a bikini.

Since rising to fame with her ex-husband on the reality series “Flip or Flop,” Christina Haack has kept her spot as one of Hollywood’s most popular TV stars. Besides her lucrative deal with HGTV, which has spawned her hit show “Christina on the Coast,” the reality star is also a mother of three.

Between filming her multiple shows, Christina tries to be available for her kids. In addition, the busy HGTV star often goes on vacations to spend quality time with her family. She also frequently takes to her Instagram to share pictures and keep her fans updated. However, one predominant feature of Christina, judging by her posts, is that the reality star loves donning a bikini. The beautiful mother of three never shies away from flaunting her figure, and her fans also can’t seem to get enough.

Nonetheless, pictures of Christina rocking a bikini always seem to send tongues wagging. In August 2021, images of Christina rocking a tiny white bikini went viral after they surfaced in the Daily Mail. The reality Tv star was on a beach in Orange County alongside her partner Joshua Hall. The Tv star soaked up the sun while donning a sports bra-like bikini top with thick straps and a thick high-waisted bottom that clung firmly to her waist.

The two-piece allowed the star to show off her toned abs and flat belly. Christina’s blonde hair parted behind her ears and ran down her shoulders as it caught the wind. In an accompanying picture, the star donned a big black trend pair of sunglasses as she stood side by side with Joshua. 

Christina’s Girls ‘n’ Bikini Photoshoot with Daughter

On March 27, 2017, the HGTV star shared a picture of her 6-year-old daughter, Taylor, and her stylishly rocking pairs of matching bikinis. The couple posed for a photoshoot for L Space Swim while wearing identical lavender bikinis. Christina also took to her Instagram to share videos of the two having a ball and playing with a hula-hoop.

They then stayed poolside while eating donuts and snuggling their French bulldog puppy, Cashie. Although the mother and daughter looked beautiful in the picture, some fans thought it was inappropriate. While some comments hailed Christina and Taylor’s bikini looks, others flagged Taylor in a bikini as‘ inappropriate.’

Those that found the beautiful photoshoot unpleasant threw shades at Christina and denounced their respect for the “Flip or Flop” star’s ‘unwise’ decision. Although Christina’s girls-bikini time photoshoot didn’t go well with a couple of fans, the pictures reflected her toned body frame in the lavender bikini, which remains one of her best. 

Christina Flaunts Her Engagement Ring in a Pink Bikini

Following the final divorce process from her ex-husband, Ant Anstead, Christina found love again with a real estate dealer Joshua Hall, and they got engaged within a noticeable period. After the engagement, the lovebirds took to a beach in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. There, the TV star wore and flaunted her 5-carat engagement ring. 

However, besides the ring, the twice-divorced TV star’s matching-two piece bikini outfit stood out in pictures shot by Paparazzi. Christina looked stunning with her athletic bikini body in full display. Meanwhile, her partner Joshua opted for broad grey shorts. 

The bright pink bikini consisted of a tiny-sleeved top with a center deep-cut, putting the star’s cleavage in full view, and a thick-fabric bottom that revealed her slightly chunky backside. The strap-laced back of the bikini top also gave the sun a chance to reflect her flawless skin. The couple also looked deep in discussions as they frolicked around in the sand. 

Christina and Joshua Get Secretly Married 

In September, Christina took to her Instagram to share a unique clip with her fans. The HGTV star shared a clip of her sons walking her down the aisle during her wedding to Joshua. The two boys, Hudson London and Brayden James, looked adorable as they walked their mother down the aisle. Christina also looked beautiful in her sheer blush gown with white floral embellishments, while her sons donned matching button-down shirts with suspenders. 

In attendance at the ceremony held at Merriman’s Kapalua in Maui, Hawaii, were the couple’s friends and family. Christina also shared a family photo where her two sons and daughter Taylor Reese stood next to her and Joshua. Joshua’s niece Audrey also joined in the family picture.

Christina’s post comes weeks after People reported the duo secretly married in California. At the time, the HGTV star also changed her last name to Hall to reflect their union. Her marriage to Joshua Hall is Christina’s third after tying the knot with Tarek El Moussa and Ant Anstead.
