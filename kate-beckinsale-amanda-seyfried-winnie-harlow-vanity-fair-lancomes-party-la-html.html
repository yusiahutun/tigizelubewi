Kate Beckinsale, Winnie Harlow and Amanda Seyfried at Vanity Fair and Lancome's party in LA

 Posted on May 4, 2024  |  9 minutes  |  1764 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kate Beckinsale had all eyes on her as she arrived to Vanity Fair and Lancome's annual Future Of Hollywood party in Los Angeles on Thursday evening.

The Underworld star, 47, was joined at the star-studded soiree by the likes of model Winnie Harlow and actress Amanda Seyfried.

It was held at the celeb-loved Italian restaurant Mother Wolf, with festivities kicking off at 6pm PST.

Star-studded: Kate Beckinsale, Winnie Harlow and Amanda Seyfried arrived to Vanity Fair and Lancome's annual Future Of Hollywood party held at Mother Wolf in Los Angeles on Thursday evening

Beckinsale hit the red carpet in a light beige Georges Chakra sleeveless jumpsuit with a stunning asymmetrical sculptural bodice with eye-catching gold leaf detailing.

It also featured a lengthy train that trailed behind the actress as she posed for shutterbugs.
