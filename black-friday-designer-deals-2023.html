The best Black Friday designer deals aren’t as easy to spot as your typical nice saves. Yes, Cyber Weekend's annual fashion deals often include savings on luxury bags and runway ready-to-wear. But designer deals worth shopping require a little more patience, and much more scrolling, to uncover.

Last week, the best Black Friday designer deals we found ranged from sumptuous leather totes by The Row to cozy wool coats from Max Mara and Toteme. We also spotted a wide selection of luxury gifts for saving on holiday shopping. Most savings were listed in department stores’ Black Friday sale sections, but a few brands also held stand-alone sales. We swept them all to bring you the best products worth shopping from the hundreds of thousands included in last year’s deals—and we’re doing it again for Cyber Monday 2023. After all, there are few bigger thrills for avid shoppers than finding their all-time favorite brands on sale.

This year, designer Cyber Monday deals come with extra, last-day discounts on an even broader range of brands. Now, the can't-miss sales are in their final hours: SSENSE, Mytheresa, and Moda Operandi have their top tier labels for up to 50 percent off; meanwhile, individual luxury brands like Altuzarra, Proenza Schouler, and Tory Burch have savings of at least 40 percent off. I also had to devote an entire article to the best Net-a-Porter Black Friday deals: There are more than 20,000 designer items on sale, with several stars that merit a place in your closet.

Get a taste of the best Cyber Monday designer deals to shop today with a selection of the top luxury sales offered for 2023 ahead. We’ll update this post with even more official Cyber Monday designer deals as soon as they're available.
