If you didn’t score a ticket to Las Vegas this year, you may be wondering how to watch BravoCon online to see panels with the casts of shows like The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, Vanderpump Rules, and Summer House. BravoCon is an annual convention hosted by Bravo featuring panels, photo-ops, exhibits, and other activities featuring cast members from almost all of Bravo’s current programs, including its 12 Real Housewives shows, five Below Deck shows, Vanderpump Rules, Summer House, Winter House, Southern Charm, and Married to Medicine.

The convention started in 2019 in New York City. The second BravoCon was held in New York City in 2022 before its move to Las Vegas for the third BravoCon in 2023. The 2023 BravoCon is at the Las Vegas Caesars Forum operated by the same company that owns Caesars Palace, the hotel that Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alum and Vanderpump Rules star Lisa Vanderpump owns her restaurant, Vanderpump Cocktail Garden.

So if you didn’t score tickets to this year’s BravoCon (or didn’t want to pay the high price for them), you may be wondering where to watch the convention online. Well, we have the answer. Read on for how to watch BravoCon online to see all your favorite Bravolebrities unite.

How to watch BravoCon 2023 online

How can Bravoholics watch BravoCon 2023 online? BravoCon is available to stream on Peacock, which offers two plans: Peacock Premium for $5.99 per month or $59.99 per year, and Peacock Premium Plus for $11.99 per month or $119.99 per year. Peacock does not have a free trial and ended its free plan in early 2023.

As for the differences between the plans, Peacock Premium includes more than 80,000 hours of TV shows, movies, and sports, as well as live sports and events, current NBC and Bravo shows, and 50 live channels. Peacock Premium Plus includes everything in Peacock Premium, as well as no ads, the ability to download and watch select titles offline, and live 24/7 access to your local NBC channel.

Peacock will stream BravoCon from the BravoVerse Live Stage each day of the three-day convention from November 3 to November 5, 2023, starting at 5:30 p.m. ET / 2:30 p.m. PT. Peacock’s BravoCon coverage also includes select panels after they end for shows like Vanderpump Rules and The Real Housewives of Atlanta, five episodes of BravoCon editions of Watch What Happens Live With Andy Cohen , interviews with Bravolebrities, and behind-the-scenes content from the convention. Though BravoCon ends on November 5, new content will be added to Peacock through November 10.

Peacock will also stream the Bravolebrity Shop Off, a one-stop shop featuring Bravolebrities pitching their most beloved products, which viewers can purchase via a QR code that will be displayed on their screens.
