FIFA 20 TOTW - What is it?

For the sake of anyone new to FIFA Ultimate team, here's a quick overview to explain how Team of the Week cards work.

The FIFA Ultimate Team 'Team of the Week', abbreviated as 'TOTW', is a weekly squad of 23 in-form players who receive an upgraded player card in Ultimate Team.

The Ultimate Team card has improved stats compared to their normal 'standard' Ultimate Team cards.

These stat increases are made by EA Sports to reflect their superior performances in the real world over the past week.

For example, if England and Spurs forward Harry Kane bangs in a hat-trick, chances are, he'll get a TOTW card with improved shooting stats.

Each Team of the Week is available to play in the game all season long, but if you want to get the FUT cards for yourself, your options are more limited.

We should see the new FIFA 20 Ultimate Team TOTW players announced at 3pm BST on September 25 (that's tomorrow) and then available from FUT packs in-game from roughly 6pm BST the same day.
