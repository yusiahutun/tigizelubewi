9 YouTube Channels to Stream While Studying

 Posted on July 28, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1194 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

You may have asked yourself at some point: “What should I listen to while studying?”

Having the right sound playing in the background can create an atmosphere that improves focus, ultimately boosts productivity, and makes your study sessions more enjoyable.

We’ve curated some of the best YouTube channels to stream while studying according to music genres, so you can choose whichever one complements your style and preference.

1. Lofi With Lofi Girl

Schoolwork can be stressful. You need something soothing to calm your nerves and also help you stay focused as you study or tackle an assignment.

Listening to some Lofi, especially from Lofi Girl on YouTube, can be a great choice. With no lyrics to distract you, the gentle tunes and melodies can provide a serene atmosphere that can elevate your mood and make studying a tad bit more pleasant.

The Studio Ghibli-inspired animations are also cute and delightful. So, if you watch YouTube in picture-in-picture mode on your primary device, it’s almost like having a study buddy, even if it’s just a cute animated girl scribbling away on her notepad.

2. Classical Music With HALIDONMUSIC

Some say classical music makes you smarter, that it’s the music of scholars, and it can improve focus so much that you can have longer study sessions and actually feel immersed in the task at hand. Some say it offers a spiritual experience that leaves you feeling inspired.

If you believe in the Mozart effect or are at least slightly interested in how music can boost your productivity, head over to HALIDONMUSIC’s channel on YouTube. There’s an extensive collection of videos, each a compilation of classic symphonies from Mozart to Chopin. Each of these videos has a theme, so you can create the perfect atmosphere when studying or struggling to hit a deadline.

3. Film Scores With Pandora Journey

You can elevate your studying session; make it a quest—something straight out of an epic movie or video game. By completing each sum, you’re leveling up— intellectually, of course. Each chapter you complete is a gremlin defeated. And when you’re scribbling an essay, you can feel like a scribe in some lore or a hero in your own academic story.

Pandora Journey on YouTube provides an extensive catalog of videos with scores that can add a touch of fantasy to your study session. There’s a reason we love watching movies and playing video games. The accompanying compositions can evoke powerful emotions and pump us full of adrenaline. So, stimulate your senses and add an element of adventure to your study session by streaming some film scores.

4. Electronic Music With Chill Music Lab

If you’re in the mood for something more upbeat while studying, consider listening to some electronic music. It can instantly put you in a good mood. And a good mood can make all the difference between a difficult and productive study session.

Chill Music Lab on YouTube might be more your style. You can either catch a live stream or choose from one of the many videos, each centered around a theme. The managers of this channel are kind enough to leave short notes of encouragement and tips in the description box to give you the kick you need to get going on some days or really just to have a better listening experience.

5. R&B With Bobby Nsenga

While some prefer silence, and others would like to have some non-lyrical music playing in the background, there are people who like to catch some good vibes when studying. You may be one of them if you prefer to hear some familiar tunes. The lyrics don’t distract you, and the beats keep you going.

However, finding the right playlist can be a bit of a struggle because creating a playlist really is an art only a few have mastered. We recommend checking out Bobby Nsenga on YouTube. He curates some of the best R&B playlists you’ll find on the internet.

6. Jazz With Coffee Relaxing Jazz

There’s just something about listening to jazz or blues that almost always calms and centers you. Some research has even linked jazz music with increasing levels of innovation. It can help reduce tension and improve clarity. If you love the genre, don’t limit your listening to a night out with friends at a bar or a cozy cafe.

You can recreate a similar atmosphere and have some beautiful, soft jazz music flowing smoothly from your speakers. Just head over to Coffee Relaxing Jazz on YouTube and choose one of their several hours-long videos.

They have videos lasting nearly 12 hours, so if you have super long study sessions, you don’t have to worry about pausing to find another video to put on. You may also want to consider getting a YouTube Premium subscription to avoid ads, which may be distracting.

7. Nature With Relaxing Sounds Of Nature

Some like to study out in nature. But you may not always have that privilege; having a park, for instance, all to yourself. Now, the Relaxing Sounds Of Nature channel on YouTube can’t recreate the warm touch of the sun.

But it can recreate the essence of nature in the private comfort of your dorm room—crickets chirping, birds calling, all of that. And you can even bring some of that essence with you to the library or anywhere you study.

8. Binaural Beats With Meditation and Healing

Often times when studying, you spend hours in silence, and your mind is focused on a particular task. This activity shares some similarities with meditation, which emphasizes being present in the moment.

Filling that silence with binaural beats, a genre of music that can help you focus, reduces anxiety levels, enhances creativity, and improves the overall quality of your study sessions. Take deep breaths, plug your ears, and dive into the reading material. You might find yourself entering a state of flow soon after.

9. Study With Merve

Studying alone can be difficult, and it’s easy to give up. Merve is a student in Scotland inviting you to join her hours-long study sessions. She shares footage of her desk as she works on research materials.

She’s super quiet; the only sounds you’ll hear are her pen touching the desk, typing sounds, and the rustle of paper as she flips pages. Occasionally, you’ll hear the birds or the rain, depending on the weather.

You don’t have to worry about studying for hours on stretch with Merve. She breaks each video into sessions, scheduling breaks at intervals. You can allocate each time block to a particular task; this can help you set clear study goals and stay productive.

Make Your Study Sessions Productive

Whether you're immersing yourself in the calming sounds of Lofi Girl, finding inspiration through classical masterpieces on HALIDONMUSIC, or embracing the upbeat energy of Chill Music Lab, these channels offer a wide range of options to enhance your study sessions.

Depending on your preferences, they can transform your study environment into a focused, soothing, or inspiring space. So, go ahead, hit play, and make the most of your study time with the power of music and ambiance that these YouTube channels have to offer.
