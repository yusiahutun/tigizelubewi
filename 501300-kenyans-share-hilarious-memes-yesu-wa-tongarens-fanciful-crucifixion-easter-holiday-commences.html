Kenyans took Easter banter to social media, sharing funny memes about self-proclaimed Jesus Christ

Eliud Wekesa, christened Yesu Wa Tongaren, grabbed the country's attention after he claimed he signified the second coming of the biblical messiah

The bearded man claimed he had performed miracles, bragging about transforming drinking water into a cup of tea

As the world marks Easter, a holiday commemorating Jesus before he was nailed on the cross, Kenyans have been cracked up by hilarious Yesu Wa Tongaren memes.

Yesu Wa Tongaren protected

On Wednesday, March 15, Yesu Wa Tongaren in an exclusive interview with TUKO.co.ke reported that the recent visit by Kenya Air Force personnel scared off his drunks, who have been threatening him.

The self-proclaimed Jesus Christ said the visit was God's way of showing his protection for his only begotten son.

"Kazi ya Mungu si unaona ni ya ajabu. Ametuma jeshi ya dunia kunitembelea. Wale walevi walikuwa wananitishia na kusulubishwa wameogopa." (God works in mysterious ways. He sent a human army to visit me. Those drunkards who were threatening me have scampered off.)
