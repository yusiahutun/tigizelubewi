Miley Cyrus Hair and Makeup for the 2015 MTV VMAs

 Posted on March 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  356 words  |  Martina Birk

If there’s one thing we expect from Miley Cyrus‘ VMAs hosting debut, it’s absolutely insane beauty looks. She’s been particularly daring over the past few years, looking more a more like she’s living life at a constant rave with each passing minute. Considering she’ll be hosting, we’re looking forward to a few outfit and beauty changes throughout the show – so clearly we had to predict what’s to come.

An extravagant, felt-adorned updo is what we think she’ll wear to walk the red carpet. This is where stars tend to bring out their most glamorous looks, so we think she’ll follow suit in her own special way. Then again, you never know when she’s just being Miley.

She made mini pigtails (and nude latex underwear) have a moment, so we wouldn’t be surprised if Cyrus brought ’em back again for the opening of the show. This time, with weed-inspired hair accessories and glittery green eye shadow. Come on, this show is guaranteed to have its fair share of controversy.

Cyrus has been favoring single rainbow extensions lately, and we predict we’ll see something similar to this simplistic, slicked-back look

…which will be paired with a rainbow manicure and pedicure, obviously.

With commercial breaks being no more than a few minutes long, those hairstylists have to work swiftly. Wigs couldn’t be a better option.

Bunny eared headbands, dreadlocks, and face tattoos seem like a Miley-approved combo for right before Justin Bieber performs.

She’s no stranger to a “no makeup” Instagram pic, and we could see her making that powerful statement at the VMAs, too.

As far as beauty looks go, we think she’ll end the show with a bang with even more glitter and feathered hair scrunchies.

Or she’ll just wear blue lipstick and an outfit totally dedicated to Drake. As one does.

But if there’s one thing we can all agree on, we’re set for one interesting show, whether that includes animal ears and rainbow hair or not. You can bet we’ll be tuning in to find out.
