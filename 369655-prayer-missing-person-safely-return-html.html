Prayer for missing person to safely return

 Posted on April 5, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1501 words  |  Martina Birk

The loss of a loved one can have a profound emotional effect on you and your family, as it leaves you living in uncertainty and hope that your loved one will one day come back. You fluctuate between being hopeful and losing hope, and this makes it difficult for you to move on. Prayer for missing person to safely return has always helped families to hold onto the hope of a happy reunion with their loved one.

If your loved one is missing, you ought to sincerely pray and talk to God so that he/she can safely return. There are no hard and fast rules as to what you are going to say during such a prayer session, so just utter what you want to say to Jesus. Let the words flow freely and let your faith believe that God will perform a miracle, and the missing person will come back home safe and sound.

Prayer for missing person to safely return

If your loved one is missing, you have faith that one day he/she will come back home safely. Here is a prayer for a lost person that will strengthen your faith and protect the missing person from trouble:

Heavenly Father, you are the wing that shelters your people. I pray for your protection over my missing loved one, for you are our hiding place, and under your wings, we have always found refuge. Protect my loved one from trouble and harm wherever he/she is. Your love and faithfulness have always surrounded me, and so I will fear no evil. Guard my loved one and protect him/her from threats. Give him/her the courage and wisdom he needs right now and shield him in your perfect love, amen.

Miracle prayer for missing person

When a loved one goes missing, the biggest hope usually left is their miraculous return. The only thing that you are left with is clinging onto hope, that this person is safe wherever they are, and that they will eventually show up. Here is a miracle prayer you can make for the missing person:

Oh, Creator of Heaven and Earth, I cry out to you on behalf of my loved one who is missing. I patiently await his/her safe return. I pray that Lord, please watch over and protect my loved one and let him/her be found in good health. Comfort me in this time of need and be the hand that will guide him/her. Send your Angels to be his/her guardian and enlighten and keep him/her safe from any harm. Lead him back home as soon as possible for nothing can be hidden from you, for you have eyes in every part of the world. It is with this reason that I entrust you with the life of my loved one that has gone missing, that he/she will return home miraculously. Amen.

Prayer for finding a missing person

It is always a prayer of every person that has a missing person that this person will return back safe and sound. Although human efforts to look for this person may prove futile, God, who is a sovereign being above all powers, has assured us that whatever we seek, as long as we have faith, will be granted unto us. Here is a prayer that will help you find your missing person

Precious Lord, I come before you at this time, to thank you for the life of my missing loved one. I lift my eyes to you; oh, gracious Lord, that you will see me through this situation. Today I cry out to you on behalf of the person who is missing. I pray that as we search for him/her oh Lord, let your light lead us to wherever he/she may be. Grant me peace that surpasses human understanding and guard my heart against negative thoughts. I pray that if my loved one is being held against his/her will, release your power and set him/her free, for nothing is impossible before you. I believe that your power will get them back to us. Amen.

Prayer vigil for a missing person

Prayer vigil for missing persons has also been known to relieve the families whose loved ones are missing. The vigil allows the family to be comforted by other people as they go through their loss. Affected families derive comfort from knowing that even though they are hurt, other people are equally affected by that loss.

The prayer vigil often represents a show of support by the community members unable to help in the search for the missing person. Normally, candles are lit in an open area which symbolizes light in the darkness of life.
