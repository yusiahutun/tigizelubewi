Meet Paula Christensen, Is It Cake? Host Mikey Day Wife

Reportedly, Mikey Day met his partner (and reportedly his wife), Paula Christensen, in 2010 way before he joined SNL. The duo has been together ever since. Paula took to Instagram on October 30, 2017, to share a sweet message on seven-year of their togetherness. She wrote, “Seven years with this gem of a human being. Feels like less because it’s still so much fun. Feels like more, because he’s my forever friend. He has a heart of gold and makes the sunshine every day. I love you @mikeyfuntime!”

The pair welcomed a child, a son, Abbott Adhemar Day, on August 17, 2012. Mikey shared a picture of the baby and baby momma on his Facebook and wrote, “Ladies, lookout. Abbott Adhemar Day. Born August 17, 2012, at 10:41 PM. 7 lbs 11 oz of cute-sauce.”

Mikey wished his son, Abbott, now 9, a happy 6th birthday by making an Instagram post in 2018.

Mikey and Paula are equally smitten with each other and Paula never hesitates to show any appreciation for how great of a guy she’s got. One time, she wrote, ” Love you, baby. So glad I get to through this leg of our journey together,” as an appreciation Instagram post for Mikey.

However, as of this writing, their relationship status was not clear meaning we did not know if the pair were still dating, engaged, or married. Since Paula does not seem to have any sort of ring in her hand in any of her Instagram pictures, it’s unlikely the couple is engaged or married.

Paula Christensen’s partner Mikey Day did his high schooling at El Modena High School and later graduated with a degree in theater from the University of California, Los Angeles.

Mikey started his career with a Los Angeles-based improvisational theater group called the Groundlings. Before Mikey began his SNL days, he wrote for Showtime’s The Underground, Cartoon Network’s Incredible Crew, Adult Swim’s Robot Chicken. He also starred in NBC’s Kath and Kim remake, NBC variety shows Maya and Marty, and hosted a recurring segment parodying TMZ from 2010 to 2013.

It was in 2013 when Mikey joined Saturday Night Live (during the 39th season) as a writer. In SNL’s 42nd season, Mikey was promoted to a featured player and repertory player in the show’s 44th season. Some of the popular celebrity impressions done by Mikey on SNL are of Travis Barker, Jack Dorsey, Steve Bannon, Matt Duffer, among others.

Paula Christensen Family

Paula Christensen moved to the United State from Buenos Aires, Argentina at the young age of 2. Although we do not know much about Paula’s dad, her Instagram paints a really good picture of how close she is to her mom. Not only Paula, but Mikey is also equally close to Paula’s mom.

Paula is the granddaughter of Carlos Hugo Christensen, one of the most prolific filmmakers in Latin American history, and Susana Freyre, another legendary Latin American actress. Carlos is known for his works such as Intruder, Three Lives in Rio, The Yacht Isabel Arrives This Afternoon, whereas Susana is best remembered for her performances in Three Lives in Rio and Leonora of the Seven Seas.
