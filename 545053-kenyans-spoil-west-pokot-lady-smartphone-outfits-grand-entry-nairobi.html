Kenyans to Spoil West Pokot Lady with Smartphone, New Outfits on Grand Entry to Nairobi

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  580 words  |  Martina Birk
  • After a heartfelt plea to Kenyans, Milka Chelyech will soon be arriving in Nairobi to visit the city for her maiden tour
  • Since her public plea, Milka caught the eye of travel firms in the city who want to take her around the city
  • Another group, identified as Kalenjins Online, is preparing to give the lady a smartphone, makeover, shopping and food for the days she will be in Nairobi

Milka Chelyech, the woman who pleaded with Kenyans to take her on a tour of Nairobi county, is expected in the city, and Kenyans are fully prepared to give her the time of her life.

Kalenjin Online Group to help Milka

Milka appeared on a news segment asking for a tour of Kitale and Nairobi towns since she has only been to Kesogon and Kenyans were touched by her simple request.

After reading the request on TUKO.co.ke, Kenyans on the Kalenjin Online Facebook group have also planned to give the humble lady a red carpet-welcome to the city.

"We sent out an SOS for people to get together and make the trip happen. The feedback has been overwhelming! Attached is a list of the pledges made so far, asanteni sana wangwana," said the admin of the group.

Milka to get KSh 15,000 phone

"We will be reaching out to these wonderful individuals to coordinate how Milka will redeem all of them. Meanwhile, if you'd like to join the bandwagon, please inbox us directly," he added.

A quick scan of the pledges shows that Milka is set to get a KSh 15,000 smartphone, free meals, flights, trips to the Nairobi National Park, new outfits, manicures, pedicures, and someone even thought of cab fees when she is around town.

Elizabeth Ngingi told TUKO.co.ke that Milka is expected in the city around April 16.

"She agreed to come and is very exhcited about the trip. She will be coming with her children for the trip, " said Milka.
