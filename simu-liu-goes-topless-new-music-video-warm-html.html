Simu Liu stripped off to reveal his toned body in the new music video for his track Warm, which was released on Wednesday.

In the clip, the 34-year-old actor goes shirtless while belting out the song's verses and getting close to model Megumi Mckenna.

The performer, who recently dropped out of Disney World's Candlelight Professional, included the track on his new EP, Anxious-Avoidant, which was released last month.
