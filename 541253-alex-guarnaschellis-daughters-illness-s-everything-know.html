Who is Alex Guarnaschelli?

Alex is an acclaimed chef, TV personality, and cookbook author. She was born on June 20, 1969, in St. Louis, Missouri, to John and Maria Guarnaschelli. Her mother was a renowned cookbook editor and publisher. Alex was born the only child to her parents.

She attended Horace Mann School and later joined Barnard College. She graduated in 1991 with a degree in art history. Alex is a skilled chef. Her culinary experience began at a young age when she watched her mother test different recipes while editing cookbooks.

Later, she decided to turn her passion into a full-time career. She started her culinary experience in France, where she enrolled at the La Varenne Cooking School in Burgundy. Alex worked under numerous French restaurants' tutelage before returning to New York City.

She was the executive chef at The Darby restaurant before its closure in 2013. Later, she landed the same position at the Butter Midtown restaurant. She is the Museum of Food and Drink's Culinary Council chairperson.
