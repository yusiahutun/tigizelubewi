Sophie Turner and Peregrine Pearson go 'Insta official' - but fans spot Joe Jonas similarity

 Posted on September 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  474 words  |  Larita Shotwell

It’s here, Sophie Turner has just gone Instagram official with her new ‘boyfriend’ Peregrine Pearson, just four months after she and Joe Jonas announced their shock divorce. However, it looks like they’re not completely out of sync yet, as the two seemingly both went away on ski trips with their new ‘love interests’.

The former celebrity couple broke hearts around the globe in September, when they announced they were going their separate ways after four years of marriage and two daughters. Since the split, Sophie Turner has been papped locking lips with the British Aristocrat, but now she’s taken it Insta official.

Sophie Turner goes ‘Insta official’ with Peregrine Pearson

Taking to her 14 million Instagram followers, Sophie did the classic ‘hard launch’ and posted pictures of herself with her new boo, on a ‘double date’ ski trip.

Although their faces were hidden with protective ski gear, the two looked close and cozy as they had their arms around each other in one snap. Others contained candid snaps of the Game of Thrones star having fun on her trip away.

The 27-year-old tagged her ‘boyfriend’ in the pics, although his account is private, so those wanting to snoop are in no such luck.

Turner’s ski trip comes just weeks after Joe Jonas was also spotted on a ski trip with his rumored new girlfriend, Stormi Bree.

The 29-year-old aristocrat has not commented on the snaps, but fans who noticed similarities to the music star’s trip have. “Joe did it first with Stormi Bree,” wrote one.

However, others were happy to see the star moving on: “Living her best life,” another commented.

Like Perry and Sophie, Joe and Stormi looked cozy and ‘loved up’ in pap pics as they were seen riding a chair-lift together in Aspen.

Joe and Stormi are yet to ‘hard launch’ however, it’s thought Joe’s sister-in-law Priyanka Chopra may have let the ‘secret’ romance slip with an Instagram post.

The actress recently posted a snap of four glasses, and eagle-eyed fans noticed one hand belonged to Stormi, from her tattoos. The post was then quickly deleted, but we all got the message.

Inside Sophie and Pergerine’s relationship

The new couple were first seen together in October 2023, a month after Turner’s divorce with Jonas, as attended the Rugby World Cup Final in Paris.

However, sources claimed the X-Men actress was just “having fun.” It had even been reported that she was ‘eager’ to meet NFL players, with the help of her good pal Taylor Swift, who’s currently dating Travis Kelce.

But in December 2023, Sophie and Peregrine were spotted on a PDA-filled outing once again. This time, on the streets of London.

Now, it looks like the couple are serious enough to ‘hard launch’ in front of millions. We wonder if Joe will follow suit anytime soon…
