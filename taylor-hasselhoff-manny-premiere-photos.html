David Hasselhoff has some good genes!

The "Baywatch" star's daughter, Taylor Hasselhoff, stole the spotlight at the Los Angeles premiere of "Manny" on Tuesday.

Sporting a very low-cut LBD and black booties, the 24-year-old beauty showed off her killer curves as she posed for pictures at the event.

You may recognize Taylor from her roles in "Sunken City," "More Than Puppy Love" and her reality show, "Meet the Hasselhoffs."

The actor certainly passed on his good looks to Taylor and her younger sister, Hayley Hasselhoff, who is an up-and-coming plus size model.

toofab's Candice Brock recently sat down with the 22-year-old bombshell to find out what The Hoff thinks of his daughter's budding career.

"He loves it! He's so supportive," Hayley gushed. "They're so proud of me, they've always told me to follow my dreams. They're all a part of it and so supportive. We're a close family, so it's really great to go back to them and tell them whats going on."

And since her proud pops has spent a good deal of time in the spotlight -- both good and bad -- he comes equipped with some pretty spot-on advice for his daughters.

"The biggest advice is really follow your dreams, follow your heart and be kind to everyone around you," she said he told her.

Life is good for the Hasselhoffs!
