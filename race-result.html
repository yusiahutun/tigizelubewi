Racing Incident Report

Horse No 1 LUCKY SWEYNESSE (G180) H Bowman Jumped awkwardly and then was crowded between WHIZZ KID and INVINCIBLE SAGE.

Despite being ridden along in the early stages failed to show sufficient pace to hold a more forward position.

Sent for sampling post-race.

Horse No 2 LUCKY WITH YOU (E356) A Atzeni After the race, A Atzeni reported that his mount did not stretch out fully on the surface which, whilst still rated as "Good" had effects of some rain which had fallen during the meeting.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 3 HOWDEEPISYOURLOVE (H093) B Avdulla From a wide barrier was shifted across behind runners in the early stages.

Horse No 4 DUKE WAI (C188) M Chadwick Shifted out at the start and was bumped.

Was left racing wide and without cover from approaching the 600 Metres.

Horse No 5 FLYING ACE (G045) Z Purton From the outside barrier was shifted across behind runners in the early stages.

Horse No 6 ADIOS (G338) K De Melo On jumping was bumped by DUKE WAI which shifted out.

Was eased down from the event from the Home Turn and went amiss near the 300 Metres.

After the race, the Veterinary Officer reported that ADIOS had bled from both nostrils.

Samples were taken from the horse.

Horse No 7 WHIZZ KID (E392) H Bentley Raced wide and without cover until approaching the 600 Metres where it was able to obtain a position outside the lead.

Horse No 8 INVINCIBLE SAGE (H301) A Badel Sent for sampling post-race.

Horse No 9 PACKING TREADMILL (G161) L Hewitson A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 11 TOMODACHI KOKOROE (H303) K Teetan After the race, K Teetan reported that his mount travelled keenly in the event and was left without a finishing effort in the Home Straight.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.
