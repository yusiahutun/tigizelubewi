Sana Khan UNFOLLOWS beau Melvin Louis On Instagram; Is all not well between the two?

 Posted on April 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  271 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Just like all the normal couples, celeb couples also go through a rough patch and end-up with misunderstandings. But, the catch here is, while we sought it out in person, anything that happens in their lives makes it to the headlines. Almost everyday we come across these celebrity couples, who give us major couple goals with their mushy pictures and cute PDA. However, we have also often seen them deleting pictures and unfollowing their beloved partners. TV stars who have followed this pattern include, Srishty Rode-Manish Naggdev, Mouni Roy-Sanjeeda Sheikh, Charu Asopa-Rajeev Sen and Amruta Khanvilkar-Himanshu Malhotra among others.
 
The latest to join the party is actress Sana Khan and her dancer-choreographer beau Melvin Louis. As per a recent report in India Forums, Sana Khan has apparently deleted pictures with Melvin and also unfollowed him on Instagram. When quipped about it, Sana out rightly rubbished the news and professed that they are very much together. Clearing air about the unfollowing Melvin on Instagram, she said that she is facing some technical glitches as she has recently changed her hand-set.

It was only a few months ago that Sana and Melvin came out in the open and officially confirmed their relationship. Since then, the much-in-love couple has been posting cute pictures of their special moments on social media. On the professional front, Sana Khan, she rose to fame after her stint in the Bigg Boss 6 house.

Well, we hope everything is really fine between the two and they keep spreading love always. What do you think about this cute couple? Do let us know in the comment section below.
