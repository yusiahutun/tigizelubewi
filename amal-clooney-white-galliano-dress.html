Amal Clooney channels her inner bride in vintage Galliano

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  301 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

As far as Amal Clooney is concerned, sequins aren't reserved for Christmas and white gowns aren't just for your wedding day.

This week, for a date night with husband George in Lake Como (where the couple have a holiday house), the human rights lawyer put a bridal spin on evening dressing.

Clooney wore a delicate vintage slip dress, from the spring/summer 1999 Christian Dior by John Galliano collection (sourced from Tab Vintage), which would not look out of place on a wedding day. The dress is fit for a bride, featuring lace ruffles, white silk, an elegant leg split and flattering thin straps. All she was missing was a veil.

Splash News

This is not the first time that Clooney has incorporated bridal white into her everyday wardrobe. Over the years, she has turned to a bridal look numerous times. In fact, she has made a habit of it.

Some of our favourites include an asymmetrical white vintage gown by Stephane Rolland that she wore a dinner at Buckingham Palace in 2019, a strapless sequin-and-feather creation by 16Arlington for the premiere of The Tender Bar in 2021 and a 1920s-inspired fringed Armani dress for an Omega event in 2019.

While Clooney is renowned for her classic and timeless approach to fashion, she is also not afraid to take risks with her eveningwear. Last month, the A-Lister wore a shimmering silver dress to a summer party, proving that sequins do not have to be reserved for the festive season. Now, she's tearing up the style book once more by wearing a bridal slip dress for date night.
