Shedeur Sanders is an American football quarterback

As seen on his ESPN profile, Dallas-born Shedeur has played for the Colorado Buffaloes since 2023. Previously, he was with the Jackson State Tigers for two seasons from 2021 to 2023.

His dad is two-time Super Bowl champion and current Colorado Buffaloes head coach – better known as “Coach Prime” – Deion Sanders. As a result, the young quarterback has access to some of the best athletes in the biz. He’s been pictured with now-retired NFL player Tom Brady, among others.

He walked in a Louis Vuitton fashion show

Shedeur has one foot in the world of high fashion too. In January, he walked in Pharrell Williams’ Louis Vuitton fashion show in Paris, France, as seen on his Instagram.

He also often shares photos of himself in designer gear, wearing brands like Louis Vuitton, Dior, Supreme, and many others.

He enjoys the high life

Shedeur clearly enjoys the finer things in life. Based on the photos he posts on his Instagram page, he likes travelling and sport, from jet-skiing on beach holidays to snowboarding.

Who is Shedeur Sanders’ mum?

Shedeur’s mother is Pilar Sanders, who was married to his father Deion from 1999 to 2013. She shares three children – Shelomi, Shedeur and Shilo Sanders – with Deion.

Deion has two other children – daughter Deiondra and son Deion Jr. – with his first wife Carolyn Chambers, per People.
