1. EasyRetro Scrum Team Name Generator

EasyRetro is a retrospective tool that helps teams improve their work. In addition to other free tools, EasyRetro has a Scrum Team Name Generator.

This simple tech team name generator asks you a few questions and then suggests a suitable team name. You have to choose an adjective, letter, number, and color relevant to your team. Moreover, it asks for your usual sprint length.

While this tool isn't specifically designed for generating tech team names, it suggests some unique names that are ideal for technical teams. For example, check out the following names generated by this tool.
