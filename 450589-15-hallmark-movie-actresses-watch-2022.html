The Hallmark Channel is well known for enjoying a monopoly of the holiday season. Whether Thanksgiving, Christmas, or Valentine, the channel has a movie or series that goes well with the current theme. But if you have followed the actors in the films, you will notice that almost all are familiar. That is because the company uses the same actors in most of its movies. So, who are the best Hallmark movie actresses?

A unique aspect about Hallmark movie actresses is that they know their way around a typical Hallmark movie set. Furthermore, many have worked with the same directors and producers and understand the scripts well.

Despite the actresses appearing in many Hallmark movies, they were also famous and starred in many blockbuster films and trending television shows. Nevertheless, they have caught many people’s eyes because of their multiple appearances in Hallmark Channel films.

15 best Hallmark movie actresses

Hallmark has many movies that sometimes can be challenging to name all. But since the channel has used the same actresses in most of its movies, pinpointing the best becomes somewhat more manageable. According to The DustBin Media, these are the best Hallmark actresses to watch:

1. Candace Cameron Bure

Candace Cameron Bure is one of the best Hallmark Christmas movie actresses. She debuted on the channel in 2011 when she played the role of Megan Walsh in The Heart of Christmas. Born on 6 April 1976 (age 46), Candace has also been in many Christmas movies on Hallmark, including:

  • Journey Back to Christmas (2016)
  • The Christmas Contest (2021)
  • A Shoe Addict's Christmas (2018)

Apart from the Christmas films, Candace Cameron Bure also plays Aurora Teagarden in the Aurora Teagarden Mystery series.

2. Lacey Chabert

Lacey Chabert is one of the top Hallmark actresses who has been in more than 20 films on Hallmark. Born on 30 September 1982 (age 39), she made her debut on the channel in 2010, portraying Liberty Taylor in Elevator Girl. Her other films include:

  • Matchmaker Santa (2012)
  • A Royal Christmas (2014)
  • Sweet Carolina (2021)

Lacey Chabert also plays Tess Harper in The Crossword Mysteries series on Hallmark.

3. Marilu Henner

Marilu Henner is one of the older Hallmark actresses and the most popular on the channel. Born on 6 April 1952 (age 70), she is best known for playing Aida Teagarden in Hallmark's Aurora Teagarden Mystery series. Furthermore, she has appeared in many films on the channel, including:

  • Love on a Limb (2016)
  • Love, Lights, Hanukkah (2020)
  • A Kiss Before Christmas (2021)

Marilu Henner also portrayed Zee Roarke in Love on a Limb (2016) on Hallmark.
