How to make Ice Magic at HOME: Woman figures out how to make the famous topping out of Nutella

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  328 words  |  Aldo Pusey

An Australian woman has revealed how to replicate Cottee's Ice Magic chocolate topping at home.

Posting the insight to the Budget Friendly Meals Australia Facebook group, Nat said she only used two ingredients, Nutella and coconut oil, to make the ice cream topping.

She said the homemade treat tasted 'delicious' over vanilla ice cream with strawberries and is the perfect 'quick fix for a sweet tooth'.

Cottee's Ice Magic is a chocolate liquid product that freezes a few seconds after being poured over ice cream. 

On Facebook, Nat said she only used two ingredients, Nutella and coconut oil, to make the homemade Ice Magic chocolate topping

To make the instant 'magic' liquid, she combined two teaspoons of Nutella and one teaspoon of coconut oil in a bowl and microwaved the mixture for 20 seconds.

The mixture froze instantly over the ice cream similarly to the store-bought version.   

The social media post swiftly received praise from hundreds of others, and many were impressed to discover the homemade method.
