What Is Joseph Sikora’s Wife’s Name? The Truth About Her Identity

Joseph lives a wonderful life with his wife Tania Sikora (née Ribalow). Although various tabloids claim that the name of Joseph’s wife is still a mystery, we know who she is. Mystery solved, ladies and gentlemen!

Who she is, Tania is a make-up artist based in New York. However, she is no ordinary make-up artist, having worked on several award-winning films and TV series.

Like her husband, she is doing great in her professional life. She has been the lead make-up artist on several projects including The Joker, The Irishman, The Greatest Showman, Notes from the Field, Broadway Empire, and others.

In addition, Joseph Sikora’s wife has been nominated for two Emmy Awards, one for The Knick and one for VINYL. She is also the recipient of the annual Make-up Artists and Hair Stylists Guild Award. She received this award together with award-winning make-up artist Nicki Ledermann.

How Did Joseph And His Partner First Meet?

The story is like something out of a romance novel. They first met on the set of Boardwalk Empire. During filming, Joseph met Tania, one of the few make-up artists on set. In many interviews, Joseph stated that he was immediately smitten by her.

In an interview with The Untitled Magazine, when asked if he ever fell in love on set, the actor said: “Head over heels! When we were shooting the pilot for Boardwalk Empire, I fell in love with the set.”

However, these feelings were not reciprocated by Tania. Joseph revealed that Tania’s film crew had a strict policy that forbade meeting actors from the same set. Apparently, her team felt that actors could be a bit selfish and self-satisfied. However, fate had other ideas.

Within a year or two, Joseph was working with many of Tania’s friends. All of them, having seen Joseph’s personality first-hand, literally begged Tania to call him, even though she didn’t. Her adamant attitude changed when her boss arranged a date for them, and her boss was none other than Nicki Ledermann, an award-winning German make-up artist.

In the same interview with The Untitled Magazine, Josef even thanked Nicki and said: “Thank you, make-up artist, extraordinaire, Nicki Ledermann!”.

Joseph’ Spouse Urged Him To Take Anger Management Classes

Joseph’s character Tommy is a favorite among the Power fandom. His interesting character design, especially the way he portrays his anger, makes him one of the best characters.

Many of you may not know that Joseph also had anger issues in real life. Yes, he even did an anger management course for five years. While he took these classes to take control of his life, they also helped him understand the anger of his character Tommy.

Moreover, it was Joseph’s wife, Tania, who encouraged him to enroll in the anger management course. In an interview with Big Boy TV, talking about his anger problems, he said: “I got into one problem when she was around and then I got into another problem when she was around”.

In addition, Sikora claimed that his wife even told him that she wanted no part of his relationship unless he went to therapy. The actor then accepted his spouse’s decision.

As he entered the class, Joseph soon began to realize that he did have a problem. He discovered,

“I was blaming other people for a lot of things instead of taking responsibility for myself and my life. It also proved that someone cared enough about me to offer this way out, and it worked.”

Joseph also said in the interview that after finding his wife, his life had a new meaning. He had found something else he loved more than acting. Life became much easier.
